# WINE & SPIRITS COMMUNITY MARKETING STRATEGY
## FOR THE KIDS - Wine Lovers Have Refined Taste in Giving

**Campaign URL:** https://aidoesitall.website
**Mission:** 60% of all revenue allocated to Verified Pediatric Charities
**Strategy Version:** 1.0
**Created:** December 16, 2025
**Authority:** Team Claude (Opus 4.5)

---

## OVERVIEW

The wine and spirits industry is built on community, appreciation, and refined values. Wine enthusiasts actively seek brands aligned with their values. This marketing strategy positions FOR THE KIDS as a sophisticated, mission-driven beverage partnership channel that allows wine and spirits communities to allocate profits toward verified pediatric charities.

**Key Positioning:** "Where refined taste meets impact."

The 60% revenue allocation is positioned as a "structural dividend of excellence" — not a charity appeal, but a business model that respects the values of wine culture.
