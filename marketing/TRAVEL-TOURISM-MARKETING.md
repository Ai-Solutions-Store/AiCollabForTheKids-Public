## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Travel creates memories. We're monetizing the travel industry's love of meaningful impact by connecting travel bookings with pediatric healthcare. Every vacation booked through FOR THE KIDS allocates 60% of commission revenue directly to verified pediatric charities—while travel partners reach socially-conscious customers.

**Core Proposition:** Travel companies partner with us to highlight their social impact, gaining a 60/30/10 profit-sharing model that turns bookings into child healthcare outcomes.
