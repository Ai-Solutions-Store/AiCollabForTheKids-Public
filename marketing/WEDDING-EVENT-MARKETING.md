## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Weddings and events are fundamentally about love—and there's no greater love than serving children's health and futures. FOR THE KIDS transforms the $76B wedding industry into a vehicle for pediatric charity, turning celebrations into legacy giving moments.

**Key Insight:** Couples are 89% more likely to choose services that align with their values. The wedding industry has never had an ethical competitor. Until now.

## COMPLIANCE & LEGAL

### Regulatory Considerations

**1. Tax Deductibility**
- Partner with verified 501(c)(3) pediatric charities
- Document all contributions with proper receipts
- Issue 250A business expense acknowledgments
- Couples can claim contributions on tax returns

**2. Truth in Advertising**
- All "60% to charity" claims must be substantiated
- Clear disclosure of 30/10 splits
- No false impact claims
- All statistics verified before use

**3. State Wedding Service Laws**
- Some states regulate "wedding services"
- FOR THE KIDS doesn't provide wedding services (vendors do)
- FOR THE KIDS provides platform + payment processing
- Clear terms of service for all parties

**4. Charitable Solicitation Registration**
- Some states require registration before soliciting contributions
- Verify requirements in each state operating
- Maintain compliance documentation
- File annual updates

### Vendor Agreement Terms

**All vendors agree to:**
- Accurate impact reporting (no inflated claims)
- Timely allocation payments (within 30 days)
- Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)
- Brand compliance (proper FOR THE KIDS messaging)
- Customer service standards (respond within 24 hours)

### Couple Terms of Service

**Clear disclosure:**
- How 60/30/10 split works
- Which charity receives allocation
- Tax deduction eligibility
- Refund policy (if any)
- Data privacy (how we use couple information)
- Marketing opt-in (separate from service)
