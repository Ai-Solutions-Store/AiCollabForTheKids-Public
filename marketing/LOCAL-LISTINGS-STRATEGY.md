## 8. NAP CONSISTENCY GUIDE (Name, Address, Phone)

### What is NAP Consistency?

NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency is the foundation of local SEO authority. Search engines like Google trust businesses with consistent information across all directories. Inconsistencies can harm rankings and confuse customers.

### FOR THE KIDS NAP Profile

#### LEGAL ENTITY NAME
```
Primary: FOR THE KIDS
Legal Entity: FOR THE KIDS Initiative / AI Solutions Store
Alternate: FOR THE KIDS Platform
DO NOT USE: "For The Kids", "For the Kids", "forthekids", "FTK"
```

#### ADDRESS OPTIONS
Option 1 (If Physical Office):
```
FOR THE KIDS
[Street Address]
[City, State ZIP]
Country: United States
```

Option 2 (If Online-Only - Use Registered Agent):
```
FOR THE KIDS
c/o Joshua Coleman
[Registered Agent Address]
[City, State ZIP]
```

Option 3 (If Operating as Part of Larger Entity):
```
FOR THE KIDS | AI Solutions Store
[Founder's Business Address]
[City, State ZIP]
```

**IMPORTANT:** Pick ONE format and use it everywhere. Do not alternate between formats.

#### PHONE NUMBER
- **Primary:** +1-XXX-XXX-XXXX (assigned for FOR THE KIDS contact)
- **Format:** Always use +1 (country code) + standard formatting
- DO NOT use: variations like (XXX) XXX-XXXX vs XXX.XXX.XXXX
- DO NOT use: different phone numbers for different directories

#### EMAIL
- **Primary:** contact@aidoesitall.website
- DO NOT use: support@, info@, hello@, or other variations
- Secondary: [founder]@aidoesitall.website (founder email)

#### WEBSITE
- **Primary URL:** https://aidoesitall.website
- **Alternate:** https://www.aidoesitall.website (use one, not both)
- DO NOT use: http:// (always https://)
- DO NOT use: different URLs on different platforms
