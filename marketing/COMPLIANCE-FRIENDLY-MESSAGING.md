## SECTION 1: CORE COMPLIANCE PRINCIPLES

### GOLDEN RULES (Non-Negotiable)

**Rule 1: Avoid "Investment" Language**
- WRONG: "Invest in pediatric charities"
- CORRECT: "Allocate to verified pediatric charities"
- Why: "Investment" implies financial return, which is regulated

**Rule 2: Separate Affiliate Disclosure**
- WRONG: "I earn commission on referrals" (buried in text)
- CORRECT: Clear, prominent disclosure at start of message
- Why: FTC requires immediate, obvious affiliate disclosure

**Rule 3: No contribution Language**
- WRONG: "Your contribution supports children"
- CORRECT: "60% of platform revenue supports pediatric charities"
- Why: "contribution" implies tax deductibility; we're revenue allocation

**Rule 4: Measurable Outcomes Only**
- WRONG: "Helps with healthcare outcomes"
- CORRECT: "Enables 300 children to receive treatment"
- Why: Vague claims invite FTC scrutiny

**Rule 5: Avoid "Guarantees"**
- WRONG: "Guaranteed 60% goes to charities"
- CORRECT: "60% is allocated to verified pediatric charities"
- Why: "Guarantee" language requires legal backing

**Rule 6: Third-Party Verification**
- ALWAYS include: Charity verification (GiveWell, Charity Navigator)
- ALWAYS include: Blockchain verification statements
- ALWAYS include: Audit trail information
