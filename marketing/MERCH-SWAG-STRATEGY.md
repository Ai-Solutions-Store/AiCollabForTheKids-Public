## STRATEGIC OVERVIEW

### The Vision

Merchandise is not just products—it's **mobile marketing and mission amplification**. Every t-shirt, hoodie, sticker, and hat worn becomes a walking billboard that:

1. **Spreads the mission** - People ask "Why does your shirt have the Core Node logo?"
2. **Drives conversation** - Opens doors to explain the 60/30/10 split
3. **Builds community** - Creates visual identity among FOR THE KIDS supporters
4. **Generates revenue** - 60% flows directly to Verified Pediatric Charities
5. **Establishes brand** - Positions FOR THE KIDS as a "Benevolent Technocracy" movement

### Merchandise Philosophy

- **Premium Quality:** No cheap drop-shipping garbage. We produce professional merchandise.
- **Mission-First Design:** Every piece tells the story of kids being helped.
- **Transparent Pricing:** Shows customers exactly how much goes to charity.
- **Community-Driven:** Limited drops, collector's editions, influencer collaborations.
- **Brand Consistency:** Core Node logo, Brand colors (Coral #CC785C, Blue #078EFA, Teal #20808D, Gold #F4B400, Green #2ECC71).

## SECTION 7: PRINT-ON-DEMAND PLATFORM STRATEGY

### Platform 1: **PRINTFUL** (Primary Production Partner)

**Why Printful:**
- Integration with e-commerce platforms
- Quality control reputation
- Fulfillment automation
- Supports all product types

**Setup:**
- API integration with Shopify store
- Whitelist Printful's fulfillment centers
- Batch printing for efficiency
- Turnaround: 3-5 business days

**Products:**
- T-shirts, hoodies, long sleeves
- Caps, beanies, hats
- Tote bags, mugs, phone cases
- Stickers, decals

**Pricing Model:**
```
PRINTFUL COST + MARKUP = RETAIL PRICE
Example: T-shirt = $4.99 (cost) + $15 (markup) = $19.99 (retail)
Margin: ~60% for wholesale, flexible for clearance
```

**Margin Allocation (Per Sale):**
- PRINTFUL: $4.99
- Retail Markup: $15.00
- **Gross Revenue: $19.99**
- 60% to Verified Pediatric Charities: $12.00
- 30% to Infrastructure: $6.00
- 10% to Founder: $2.00
