- Middle touches (consideration)
- Last touch (conversion)

**Report weekly on:**
- Top 3 first-touch channels (awareness drivers)
- Top 3 last-touch channels (conversion drivers)
- Most common conversion paths

## VIRAL COEFFICIENT CALCULATION (K-Factor)

**Formula:**
```
K = (Number of invitations sent per user) Ã— (Conversion rate of invitations)
```

**Goal:** K > 1.0 (exponential growth)
- K = 1.0 means each backer brings 1 more backer (linear growth)
- K > 1.0 means exponential growth
- K < 1.0 means decay (need more marketing)

### How to Calculate

**Step 1: Track Referrals**
- Kickstarter has built-in referral tracking
- Each backer gets unique referral link
- Track how many people they share with

**Step 2: Measure Invitation Rate**
```
Invitations per user = Total shares / Total backers
```
**Target:** 5+ shares per backer

**Step 3: Measure Invitation Conversion**
```
Invitation conversion = Referral backers / Total shares
```
**Target:** 15-20%

**Step 4: Calculate K-Factor**
```
K = 5 shares Ã— 0.15 conversion = 0.75
```

### Improving K-Factor

**Increase invitations per user:**
- Make sharing easy (one-click)
- Incentivize sharing (unlock bonus content at X shares)
- Gamify (leaderboard of top referrers)
- Thank backers publicly, encourage sharing

**Increase conversion rate:**
- Strong social proof in referral landing page
- Clear value proposition
- Urgency/scarcity messaging
- Testimonials from early backers

**Target K-Factor by Week:**
- Week 1: K = 0.5 (building)
- Week 2: K = 0.75 (growing)
- Week 3: K = 1.0 (viral threshold)
- Week 4: K = 1.2+ (exponential)

## APPENDIX: METRIC DEFINITIONS

**Reach:** Number of unique people who saw the content
**Impressions:** Total number of times content was displayed (includes repeats)
**Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach
**Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Clicks / Impressions
**Conversion Rate:** Backers / Clicks
**Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Total Spend / Backers (should be $0 for organic)
**Average Order Value (AOV):** Total Revenue / Backers
**K-Factor:** Viral coefficient = (Invites per user) Ã— (Conversion rate of invites)
**North Star Metric:** The one metric that matters most (Funds to Kids = Total Ã— 60%)
