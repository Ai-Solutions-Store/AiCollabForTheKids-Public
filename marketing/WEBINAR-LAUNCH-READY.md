## Q&A HANDLING PROTOCOLS

**BEFORE THE WEBINAR**

   - Registration form includes: "What would you like to ask?"
   - 48 hours before webinar: Email reminder "Submit your questions here"
   - Questions submitted ? Reviewed by speaker 24 hours before

2. **Pre-Screen Questions**
   - Identify: Top 5 most-asked questions
   - Prepare: Speaker practices answers (2-3 min each)
   - Organize: Group similar questions

3. **Prepare Warm-Up Questions**
   - Create 2-3 "easy" questions to start (build rapport)
   - Example: "What makes this different from Khan Academy?"
   - These are pre-screened, not live chat

---

**DURING THE WEBINAR**

1. **Warm-Up Q&A (First 3-5 min)**
   - Use pre-screened questions
   - Speaker answers in 2-3 minutes each
   - Builds rapport, gets audience comfortable

2. **Live Chat Monitoring**
   - Moderator reads live chat in real-time
   - Flag questions by category: Technical / Clarification / Gospel / Product
   - Surface best questions to speaker

3. **Speaker Answers Live**
   - Speaker calls on top 5-7 questions
   - Answers in 2-3 min each
   - Refers to earlier slides if needed ("We covered this on slide 14")

4. **Unanswered Questions**
   - Moderator notes: "We didn't get to [QUESTION]. We'll answer in follow-up email."
   - Commitment: "You'll get written answers to all questions within 24 hours."

---

**AFTER THE WEBINAR**

1. **Q&A Transcript**
   - Compile all questions (asked + unanswered)
   - Write full answers for unanswered questions
   - Format: Clean PDF document
   - Send: Within 24 hours via email

2. **FAQ Document**
   - Top 20 questions + answers
   - Add to website
   - Repurpose as blog posts

3. **One-on-One Follow-up**
   - Complex questions: Offer 1:1 call with expert
   - Technical questions: Forward to product/engineering team
   - Partnership questions: Forward to business dev
