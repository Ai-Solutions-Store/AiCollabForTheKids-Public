### Testing Framework

#### Primary Metrics
- **CTR (Click-Through Rate):** % of impressions that click
- **CPC (Cost Per Click):** Ad spend ÷ total clicks
- **Conversion Rate:** % of clicks that become Kickstarter backers
- **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):** Kickstarter revenue ÷ ad spend
- **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition):** Ad spend ÷ number of backers

#### Secondary Metrics
- **Engagement Rate:** Likes, shares, comments
- **Video Completion Rate:** % watching full video ad
- **Landing Page Bounce Rate:** % leaving Kickstarter page immediately
- **Average Pledge Amount:** Quality of conversions

#### Success Benchmarks
- **CTR Target:** 2.5%+ (Google), 1.5%+ (Facebook), 0.8%+ (YouTube)
- **Conversion Rate Target:** 5%+ (ad click ? Kickstarter backer)
- **ROAS Target:** 3:1 minimum ($3 in pledges per $1 in ad spend)
- **CPA Target:** <$15 per backer
