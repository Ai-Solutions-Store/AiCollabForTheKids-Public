## DEPLOYMENT STRATEGY

### Timing
- **Message 1:** Launch day (first 2 hours)
- **Message 2:** 48 hours before deadline (scarcity peak)
- **Message 3:** Mid-campaign (day 7-10, re-engagement)
- **Message 4:** VIP list only (exclusive early access)
- **Message 5:** Final 6 hours (last chance push)

### Segmentation
| Segment | Best Message | Send Time |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| Warm leads (engaged but not backed) | Message 2 or 5 | Evening (6-8pm local) |
| Tech community contacts | Message 3 | Weekday lunch (12-1pm) |
| VIP/influencer list | Message 4 | Morning (9-10am) |
| Cold prospects (first touch) | Message 1 | Avoid - SMS for warm only |
| Previous donors/charity supporters | Message 1 or 5 | Weekend afternoon (2-4pm) |

### Compliance Notes
- **Opt-in required:** Only send to contacts who explicitly opted in
- **Opt-out included:** All messages should include opt-out language (reduce char count if needed)
- **Rate limiting:** Max 1 SMS per contact per 48 hours
- **Link shortening:** Use bit.ly or custom domain (track clicks)
