## PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The Healthcare Professional Outreach Program is a comprehensive, multi-channel strategy to engage nurses, pharmacists, and all healthcare workers with FOR THE KIDS' mission-driven platform.

**Core Premise:** Healthcare professionals dedicate their careers to child and patient health. Their technology investments should reflect that mission, with 60% of revenue flowing directly to pediatric charities.

**Step 3: Customize and Send**
- Select appropriate email template
- Customize with specific names, organizations, details
- Verify all placeholder text is replaced
- Send using your email/communication tool

**Step 4: Track and Measure**
- Log all outreach in partnership tracking dashboard
- Record responses and next steps
- Update monthly metrics
- Report to leadership

### For Marketing & Social Media Team

**Step 1: Review Content Calendar**
- Check HEALTHCARE-SOCIAL-MEDIA-CONTENT.md for seasonal calendar
- Identify which platforms your audience uses most
- Plan content schedule for next 90 days

**Step 2: Customize Content**
- Select posts matching your target audience
- Add images, graphics, or videos as needed
- Replace placeholder text ([Organization Name], etc.)
- Adjust tone if needed for your audience

**Step 3: Schedule Posts**
- Use social media management tool (Buffer, Hootsuite, etc.)
- Schedule 2-4 weeks in advance
- Use hashtags and posting times from document
- Plan engagement responses

**Step 4: Track Performance**
- Monitor engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares)
- Track click-through to aidoesitall.website/healthcare
- Collect and measure signup conversion
- Report monthly performance

### For Leadership & Partners

**Step 1: Review Strategic Plan**

- Review budget allocation and revenue projections
- Understand quarterly roadmap and milestones

**Step 2: Monitor Key Metrics**
- Healthcare professional signups (monthly)
- Organization partnerships signed

**Step 3: Support Outreach Team**
