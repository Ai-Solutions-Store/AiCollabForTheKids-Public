## 8. HASHTAG RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

### Step 1: Seed Hashtag Collection
**Sources:**
- Competitor analysis (10-20 similar social enterprises)
- Charity sector leaders (Red Cross, UNICEF, local hospitals)
- Parenting influencers (top 50 in niche)
- Tech startup communities (ProductHunt, IndieHackers)

**Tool:** Spreadsheet with columns:
- Hashtag
- Platform
- Volume (posts)
- Engagement rate
- Competition level
- Relevance score (1-10)

### Step 2: Volume & Competition Analysis
**Optimal Hashtag Profile:**
- **High Volume (1M+ posts):** 20% of hashtags - Brand awareness
- **Medium Volume (100K-1M posts):** 60% of hashtags - Targeted reach
- **Low Volume (10K-100K posts):** 30% of hashtags - Niche community

**Competition Levels:**
- Low: Less than 100K posts (easier to rank)
- Medium: 100K-1M posts (moderate ranking chance)
- High: 1M+ posts (difficult to rank, but high exposure)

**Tools:**
- Instagram search (see post count)
- Hashtagify (competition score)
- Display Purposes (hashtag generator + analysis)

### Step 3: Relevance Scoring
**Score each hashtag 1-10 based on:**
- **Mission alignment** (Does it reflect Gospel V1.3 values?)
- **Audience match** (Are our target users using this tag?)
- **Content fit** (Can we authentically create content for this tag?)
- **Brand safety** (Is this tag free of controversy/spam?)

**Minimum relevance score:** 6/10 to include in strategy

### Step 4: Engagement Testing
**A/B Test Hashtag Sets:**
- Week 1: Set A (broad reach focus)
- Week 2: Set B (niche community focus)
- Week 3: Set C (hybrid approach)
- Week 4: Best performing set from Weeks 1-3

**Metrics to track:**
- Reach from hashtags
- Engagement rate on hashtagged posts
- New followers from hashtag discovery
- Profile visits from hashtag clicks

### Step 5: Quarterly Refresh
**Every 90 days:**
- Review hashtag performance data
- Retire bottom 20% performers
- Research new emerging hashtags
- Update hashtag sets based on data

## 10. HASHTAGS TO AVOID

### Overused/Saturated (Low ROI)
**Why avoid:** Too much competition, impossible to rank, low engagement

```
#Love #Instagood #PhotoOfTheDay #Beautiful #Happy #Like4Like #Follow4Follow
#InstaDaily #FollowMe #TBT #InstaMood #Selfie #Amazing #Style #Smile
#Life #Fun #Cool #Awesome #Swag #Motivation #Inspiration #Success #Fitness
```

**Note:** These have 100M-1B posts. Your content will be buried in seconds.

### Banned/Shadowbanned by Instagram
**Why avoid:** Will suppress your post reach, can hurt account standing

```
#Snapchat #SingleMom #Porn #Booty #Valentinesday #Fitnessgirls #Brain
#Gloves #Elevator #Tanlines #Killingit #Hustler #Skype #Bikinibody
#BestOfTheDay #Instasport #Instahealthy #Instacool #Instahappy
```

**Note:** Instagram's banned list changes. Check: https://www.instagram.com/explore/tags/[hashtag]
If it says "No posts yet" but Google shows millions of uses = shadowbanned

### Controversial/Political
**Why avoid:** Brand safety risk, potential alienation of audience

```
#MAGA #Resist #ProLife #ProChoice #GunControl #2A #BorderWall
#Socialism #Capitalism #Trump2024 #Biden2024 [Any political candidate]
```

**Exception:** Bipartisan child welfare policy hashtags MAY be acceptable if highly relevant and non-divisive.

### Spam/Bot Tags
**Why avoid:** Attract spam accounts, low-quality engagement

```
#FollowBack #InstaFollow #FollowForFollow #LikeForLike #Like4Like #F4F #L4L
#TeamFollowBack #FollowTrain #InstaFollowers #FollowBackAlways #GainFollowers
#GainPost #GainParty #GainWithSpam #GainTrick #GainFollowersQuick
```

**Note:** These violate most platform TOS and can get you flagged.

### Irrelevant Popular Tags (Hashtag Stuffing)
**Why avoid:** Hurts credibility, annoys users, algorithm penalizes

**Example of what NOT to do:**
```
Post: "Check out our new charity platform! #ForTheKids"
Bad hashtags: #FitnessModel #FoodPorn #Travel #Sunset #Beach #Party #OOTD
```

**Rule:** Only use hashtags directly related to your post content.

### Overly Broad Single-Word Tags
**Why avoid:** Too generic, low conversion, wrong audience

```
#Kids #Family #Charity #Tech #Business #Money #App #Website #Internet
#Social #Media #Online #Digital #Platform #System #Service #Product
```

**Better alternative:** Use specific 2-3 word phrases
- Instead of #Kids → #RaisingKids #KidsFirst #HealthyKids
- Instead of #Charity → #CharityAccountability #PediatricCharity #TransparentGiving

### Hashtag Spam Indicators
**Red flags that a hashtag is spam:**
- Used by accounts with suspicious follower ratios (10K following, 100 followers)
- Comments on posts are generic ("Great pic!" "Love this!" "Follow me!")
- Majority of posts are promotional/sales focused
- Hashtag has no clear theme or community

## 14. HASHTAG GOVERNANCE & GUIDELINES

### Who Can Create Branded Hashtags?
**Approval Required:**
- New campaign hashtags: Marketing Director approval
- Permanent branded hashtags: Founder + Marketing approval
- Event-specific hashtags: Campaign Manager approval

**No Approval Needed:**
- Industry/niche hashtags from approved list
- Trending hashtags (must follow guidelines)
- Community hashtags (parenting, tech, etc.)

### Hashtag Naming Conventions
**Rules for branded hashtags:**
1. **Clear & Memorable:** Easy to spell, type, remember
2. **Unique:** Google/Instagram search shows minimal existing use
3. **Brand Aligned:** Reflects Gospel V1.3 values
4. **Appropriate:** Family-friendly, PG-rated
5. **Short:** Ideally 15 characters or less
6. **No Numbers/Special Characters:** Unless part of brand (#60ForKids is OK)

**Good Examples:**
- `#ForTheKids` (Clear, short, memorable)
- `#60ForKids` (Unique, brand-specific)
- `#BackForTheKids` (Actionable, campaign-specific)

**Bad Examples:**
- `#ForTheKidsCharityPlatform2025Launch` (Too long)
- `#4TK` (Unclear, uses number substitution)
- `#For-The-Kids` (Special characters don't work)

### Brand Voice in Hashtags
**Tone Guidelines:**
- Empowering (not victim-mentality)
- Transparent (not vague)
- Action-oriented (not passive)
- Inclusive (not exclusive)
- Hopeful (not guilt-trippy)

**Examples:**
- ✅ `#KidsFirstAlways` (Empowering)
- ❌ `#SaveTheKids` (Victim-mentality)
- ✅ `#TransparentGiving` (Transparent)
- ❌ `#TrustUs` (Vague)

### UGC Hashtag Moderation
**Monitor user-generated content on branded hashtags:**
- Daily scan of #ForTheKids, #60ForKids, #BackForTheKids
- Flag inappropriate use (spam, off-brand, NSFW)
- Engage with positive UGC (like, comment, repost)
- Report/block bad actors

**Response Protocol:**
- **Positive UGC:** Like, comment "Thank you for joining the movement!"
- **Spam:** Report, do not engage
- **Off-brand but harmless:** Ignore
- **Inappropriate:** Report, block if necessary, document
