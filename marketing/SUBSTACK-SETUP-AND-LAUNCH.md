## PHASE 1: ACCOUNT SETUP (Days 1-3)

### Step 1: Create Substack Account

1. Go to https://substack.com/
2. Click "Start writing" (top right)
3. Enter email + create password
4. Verify email address

### Step 2: Configure Basic Settings

**Publication Name:** "The Core Node" (recommended from strategy)

**Publication Description:**
> "AI-driven stories. Verified impact. Building a platform where 60% of revenue flows to pediatric charities. Welcome to the mission."

**Publication URL:**
- If available: `thecorenodes.substack.com`
- Alternative: `forthekinds.substack.com`

### Step 3: Upload Branding

**Logo:**
- Use FOR THE KIDS Core Node isometric heart icon
- Dimensions: 1200x1200 px (square)
- Format: PNG or JPG

**Cover Image:**
- Blockchain visualization + kids imagery (tasteful blend)
- Dimensions: 1500x800 px
- File size: < 2 MB

**Color Scheme:**
- Primary: Coral (#CC785C) - warmth, human connection
- Secondary: Blue (#078EFA) - trust, technology
- Accent: Teal (#20808D) - innovation

### Step 4: Write Publication Bio (300 words max)

```
FOR THE KIDS is a mission-driven platform where 60% of every dollar
goes directly to verified pediatric charities.

We're not a typical newsletter. We're an experiment in proving that
business can be genuinely mission-aligned without compromise.

Every article explores the intersection of:
- Blockchain transparency for charity
- AI and effective altruism
- Building platforms where profit and purpose align
- Gospel V1.3: The revenue model where kids come first

Join 10,000+ readers who believe technology should serve humanity.

Subscribe free for weekly updates on impact, tech, and the future
of mission-driven business.

Upgrade to paid ($10/month) for exclusive deep-dives, monthly AMAs
with Joshua (founder), and access to our community of 500+ mission-aligned subscribers.

All revenue is allocated per Gospel V1.3:
- 60% ? Verified Pediatric Charities
- 30% ? Infrastructure
- 10% ? Founder

Verify our allocation: Smart contract Planned (Q1 2026) on Base Mainnet
```

### Step 5: Set Up Payment (for Paid Subscriptions)

**Monthly at 500 paid subscribers ($5,000 revenue):**

| Item | Amount | Allocation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Total Revenue** | $5,000 | 100% |

- Substack fee (10% of revenue): $485
- Email delivery/monitoring: $50
- Slack workspace (premium): $50

- Reserves for scaling: $771

### Pricing Strategy

**Current pricing:** $10/month or $100/year (locked in for founding subscribers)

**Q2 2026 increase:** $15/month or $150/year
- Grandfather clause: Founding members keep $10/month forever
- New subscribers at $15/month

**Rationale:**
- Increased costs as community grows
- Paid community management
- Video production for AMAs
- Charity partnership development
