KEY SUCCESS FACTORS
================================================================================

1. AUTHENTICITY
   - Only pitch athletes who genuinely care about kids' health
   - Research their foundation/charitable history first
   - Personalize every outreach

2. SIMPLICITY
   - Make athlete participation minimal (3-5 social posts max)
   - No complex compliance requirements
   - Pre-approve all creative assets (48-hour turnaround)

3. IMPACT VISIBILITY
   - Show real-time impact dashboards
   - Kids helped = concrete metric
   - Monthly reports to partners

4. BRAND ALIGNMENT
   - FOR THE KIDS = "Benevolent Technocracy"
   - Serious tech firm that happens to help kids
   - NOT charity-first positioning
   - Sports positioning: "Where champions support children"

5. SCALE THINKING
   - One athlete = millions reached
   - One team = millions reached
   - Cumulative effect multiplies exponentially
   - Focus on volume partnerships, not perfection
