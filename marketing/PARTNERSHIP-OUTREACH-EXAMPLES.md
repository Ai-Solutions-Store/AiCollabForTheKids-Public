## TEMPLATE ADAPTATION GUIDE

### How to Turn These Examples Into Your Own:

**Step 1: Research (15 minutes per prospect)**
- LinkedIn profile (find contact name, title)
- Company website (recent news, values page)
- Recent press releases (new products, awards, expansions)
- Social media (what they post about, community involvement)

**Step 2: Find the Connection (5 minutes)**
- How does our mission align with their values?
- Which product would actually help their business/mission?
- What specific pain point can we address?
- Why now? (recent event, expansion, new initiative)

**Step 3: Customize the Template (10 minutes)**
- Replace ALL [PLACEHOLDERS] with specific details
- Add 1-2 sentences unique to this prospect
- Include concrete numbers relevant to their scale
- Tailor CTA to their situation (call, trial, coffee)

**Step 4: Proofread (2 minutes)**
- Check for [BRACKET PLACEHOLDERS] still in text
- Verify all names/companies spelled correctly
- Confirm links work (aidoesitall.website, etc.)
- Read out loud for tone (friendly but professional)

**Total Time Per Email: 32 minutes** (worth it for high-value prospects)
