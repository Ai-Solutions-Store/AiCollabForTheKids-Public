## Design Notes

### Color Palette
- Primary: Deep blue (#1a237e) - Trust and stability
- Accent: Bright green (#00c853) - Growth and hope
- Alert: Bold orange (#ff6f00) - Urgency and action
- Background: Clean white (#ffffff) - Clarity and transparency

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Leaderboard:** Single line headline (bold), inline subhead, prominent CTA button
- **Medium Rectangle:** 2-line headline possible, 1-2 line subhead, centered CTA
- **Skyscraper:** Multi-line headline, stacked subheads/bullets, bottom-aligned CTA

### Visual Elements
- Include "60%" as large, prominent number where space allows
- Gospel V1.3 badge/seal (small, corner placement)
- Blockchain verification icon (trust signal)
- Child-friendly imagery (ethical, hopeful, not exploitative)

---

## A/B Testing Strategy

### Test Variables
1. **Message Focus:** Impact vs. Value vs. Trust
2. **CTA Language:** "Shop Now" vs. "Start Giving" vs. "Join Movement"
3. **Number Prominence:** Large "60%" vs. text-based percentage
4. **Urgency Level:** Calm/aspirational vs. urgent/actionable

### Success Metrics
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversion rate
- Time on site post-click
- Cart abandonment rate

## Compliance Notes

All banner ads MUST:
- Display accurate 60% charitable giving percentage (Gospel V1.3)
- Link to verified charity documentation
