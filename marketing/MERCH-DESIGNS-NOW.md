## PRINT SPECIFICATIONS MASTER REFERENCE

### Universal Print Standards

**Quality Requirements:**
- Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI for all prints
- Color profile: CMYK for print, RGB for digital mockups
- File formats: Vector (AI, EPS, SVG) for logos/text, high-res raster (PNG, TIFF) for detailed art
- Bleed: 0.125" to 0.25" depending on item
- Safe area: 0.25" margin from cut line

**Ink Types:**
- **Plastisol:** Durable, bright colors, good for cotton
- **Water-based:** Soft feel, eco-friendly, absorbs into fabric
- **Discharge:** Removes dye, replaces with color, soft vintage feel
- **DTG (Direct-to-Garment):** Full color, complex designs, photo-quality
- **Screen Print:** Best for solid colors, cost-effective for bulk
- **Sublimation:** For mugs/ceramics, permanent, vibrant
- **Embroidery:** Premium feel, textured, durable

**Testing Requirements:**
- Wash test: 50+ cycles for apparel
- Fade test: UV exposure for outdoor items
- Scratch test: Adhesion for stickers/mugs
- Stretch test: Flexibility for apparel prints
