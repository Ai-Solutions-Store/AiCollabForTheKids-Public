## SECTION 1: AMBASSADOR PROGRAM STRUCTURE

### Program Tiers

#### Tier 1: **The Advocate** (Entry Level)
**Requirements:**
- Share 1+ campaign post per month
- Attend 1 company advocacy event
- Follow FOR THE KIDS social accounts

**Perks:**
- Exclusive "Advocate" badge for email signature
- Digital Advocate Certificate
- Monthly recognition in internal newsletter
- 5% discount on FOR THE KIDS merchandise
- Entry into quarterly raffle ($100 gift card)

**Time Commitment:** 15-30 minutes/month

---

#### Tier 2: **The Champion** (Active Advocate)
**Requirements:**
- Share 3+ campaign posts per month
- Attend 2+ company advocacy events
- Refer 3+ employees to join program
- Participate in 1 team fundraising campaign

**Perks:**
- "Champion" badge + LinkedIn banner
- Quarterly spotlight in company newsletter
- $50 gift card per month (4x/year = $200)
- Priority invitation to exclusive FOR THE KIDS events
- 10% discount on merchandise
- Name featured on "Champions Wall" (digital)
- Semi-annual team lunch with leadership

**Time Commitment:** 1-2 hours/month

---

#### Tier 3: **The Beacon** (Leadership)
**Requirements:**
- Share 5+ campaign posts per month
- Host 1 internal awareness event
- Mentor 2+ new advocates
- Lead 1 company fundraising campaign
- Achieve 100+ referral clicks per month

**Perks:**
- "Beacon" title (company directory & email)
- Monthly $100 gift card (or charitable contribution on their behalf)
- Annual appreciation dinner with founder
- Dedicated Slack channel: #beacons-of-hope
- Feature in company press/marketing materials
- 15% merchandise discount
- Quarterly 1-on-1 with leadership
- "Beacon" branded company swag

**Time Commitment:** 2-4 hours/month

---

#### Tier 4: **The Architect** (Founder Level)
**Requirements:**
- Become certified volunteer coordinator
- Fundraise $5,000+ annually
- Lead 2+ major initiatives
- Publish 1+ thought leadership piece
- 10+ referrals who reach Champion tier

**Perks:**
- "Architect" title (significant achievement)
- Annual $1,500 charitable contribution in their name to pediatric charity of choice
- Quarterly strategic planning sessions with leadership
- Board-level quarterly briefings
- Feature in annual impact report
- Named sponsorship of company initiative
- VIP access to all FOR THE KIDS events
- Executive merchandise package

**Time Commitment:** 4-6 hours/month
