3. **Scalability** - Can grow allocations from $1M to $100M+
4. **Non-correlated impact** - Pediatric health isn't tied to market cycles
5. **Long-term value creation** - Healthier children = economic productivity over 50+ years

### COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE vs. TRADITIONAL IMPACT INVESTING

**FOR THE KIDS asks:** "What if your allocation IS the social outcome?"
