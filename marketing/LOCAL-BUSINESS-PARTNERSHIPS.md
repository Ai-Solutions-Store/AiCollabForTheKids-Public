## STRATEGIC OVERVIEW

### The Opportunity

**60% of Revenue Allocates to Verified Pediatric Charities**

Local businesses want to do good without major operational overhead. Our partnership model lets them participate in child welfare while maintaining their core business.

**Why Local Businesses Care:**

- Community goodwill and loyalty
- Tax deductibility for business partners
- Social media engagement opportunities
- Differentiation in competitive markets
- Built-in customer storytelling

**Why FOR THE KIDS Works:**

- Simple, transparent 60/30/10 revenue split
- No complex treasury or charity infrastructure needed
- Customers purchase products/services (not "contributing")
- Real impact: ~$2,000 = one child's pediatric care
- Blockchain transparency via smart contract
