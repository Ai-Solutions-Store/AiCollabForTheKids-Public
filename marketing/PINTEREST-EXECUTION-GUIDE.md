### 4.4 Image Quality & Optimization Guidelines

**Photography Standards:**
- **Stock Photos:** Use Unsplash, Pexels (free) or Shutterstock (premium)
- **Recommended Subjects:** Diverse faces (parents, teachers, business owners), healthcare settings, technology/laptop scenes, nature/growth imagery
- **Avoid:** Clipart, cartoons (unless brand-specific), outdated stock imagery, stereotypical diversity photos

**Accessibility Standards:**
- Minimum text contrast ratio: 4.5:1 (WCAG AA)
- Alt text required for all pins (descriptive, 10-15 words)
- Color should not be only differentiator (use icons + labels)
- Font minimum: 12pt for body text when readable

**Processing & Effects:**
- Slight dark overlay on photos for text readability (20-30% opacity)
- Boost saturation on colors to pop against dark background
- Add subtle drop shadow behind text (2-4px, 20% opacity black)
- No heavy filters or distortion

**Performance:**
- Final PNG files: 200-300KB maximum
- JPEG files: 150-250KB maximum
- Test on slow mobile connections
- Verify text readability on 6" mobile screen

---

### 4.5 Text Treatment & Readability

**Hierarchy Rules:**
1. **Headline** (most prominent, 48-72pt)
   - Primary message
   - High contrast with background
   - Bold weight only
   - All caps or Title Case (never lowercase)

2. **Subheading** (secondary emphasis, 28-36pt)
   - Supporting message or number
   - Same font family as headline
   - Regular weight
   - One color accent maximum

3. **Body Text** (supporting details, 14-20pt)
   - 2-3 lines maximum
   - Regular or light weight
   - High contrast (white on dark)
   - One concept per line when possible

4. **CTA Text** (action prompt, 16-24pt)
   - Bold weight
   - Contrasting color (Coral #CC785C preferred)
   - Action-oriented verb ("Discover," "Learn," "Start," not "Click Here")

**Text Placement Rules:**
- Headline: Top third, centered or left-aligned
- Body: Middle section, centered or left-aligned
- CTA: Bottom fifth, centered with visual emphasis
- Never place critical text in outer 50px margins

**Color Combinations (Tested for Contrast):**

### 4.8 Common Mistakes to Avoid

? **Text Too Small**
- Can't read on mobile
- Pinterest clips text over 1000px
- Fix: Test on 6" phone screen

? **Low Contrast**
- Colors blend into background
- Unreadable for accessibility
- Fix: Use contrast checker tool (WebAIM)

? **Busy Design**
- Too many elements compete
- Pins fail to stop the scroll
- Fix: Maximum 3 focal points per pin

? **Horizontal Layout**
- Pinterest crops to 2:3 aspect ratio
- Important content gets cut off
- Fix: Always design for vertical 1000x1500px

? **Missing CTA**
- Unclear what action to take
- Engagement drops 40%
- Fix: Include 1 clear CTA per pin

? **Dated Stock Photos**
- Looks like 2015 marketing
- Reduces credibility
- Fix: Use recent, diverse, authentic imagery

? **Brand Color Inconsistency**
- Uses corporate blue instead of FOR THE KIDS palette
- Dilutes brand recognition
- Fix: Use color picker to match exact hex values

? **Small Logo/Attribution**
- People don't know who created it
- Loses brand association
- Fix: Logo minimum 60px width
