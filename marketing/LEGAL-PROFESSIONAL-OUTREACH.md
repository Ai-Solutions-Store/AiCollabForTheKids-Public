Lawyers are problem-solvers with deep family connections. When they see a platform where 60% of revenue directly helps sick children at verified pediatric charities, they respond.

**The Math:** A lawyer who generates $10,000 in platform referrals or partnerships = $6,000 directly helping children in real hospitals. That's measurable. That's powerful.
