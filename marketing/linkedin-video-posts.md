This is AI as institutional memory. As ethical guardrail. As enforcement mechanism for values we claim but might abandon when convenient.

**The Result:**

I took a 10% pay cut to increase charity allocation by 10%. I sleep better knowing an AI board with no financial incentive prevents me from ever reversing that decision.

This is what AI alignment looks like in production. Not AGI safety theory - practical implementation of machine-enforced human values.

- **Tuesday-Thursday:** 9-11 AM EST (highest engagement)

- **Avoid:** Weekends, early mornings (before 7 AM), late evenings (after 6 PM)

### Video Specifications
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (landscape) or 1:1 (square) preferred
- **Length:** 30 seconds - 3 minutes (LinkedIn native video)
- **File Size:** Max 5GB
- **Captions:** ALWAYS include (85% watch without sound)

1. **Hook (Line 1):** Must capture attention in feed preview
