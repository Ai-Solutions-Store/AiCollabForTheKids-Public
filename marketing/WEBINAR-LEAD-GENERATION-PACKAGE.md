## SECTION 4: CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION TIPS & LEAD GENERATION STRATEGIES

### A. LANDING PAGE OPTIMIZATION

#### 1. Mobile-First Design
- 65% of webinar registrations come from mobile
- Ensure forms work perfectly on small screens
- Single-column layout (no multi-column on mobile)
- CTA buttons: 48px minimum height (easy thumb-tap)
- Load time under 3 seconds on 4G

#### 2. Form Field Optimization
- Reduce form fields: Each field = 2% drop in conversions
- Required fields: First name, email, role only
- Optional fields: Phone, company, kids' ages (use progressive profiling)
- First name field: Auto-focus (cursor already there)
- Disable autocomplete on email (prevents browser pre-fill errors)

#### 3. Social Proof Placement
- Place testimonials ABOVE signup form (not below)
- Use video testimonials (3x higher conversion than text)
- Include participant count: "3,247 registered for this webinar"
- Include attendee count from previous webinars: "[X] attended live last month"
- Show real photos of attendees (not stock photos)

#### 4. CTA Button Optimization
- Color: High contrast (blue/orange on light backgrounds)
- Text: Action-oriented ("Register Now" not "Submit")
- Size: Large enough to be obvious, not so large it dominates
- Animation: Subtle pulse or hover effect (but don't overdo)
- Test copy variations:
  - "Register Now (Free)" vs. "Yes, Add Me"
  - "Reserve My Spot" vs. "Save My Seat"
  - Track which converts better

#### 5. Headline Testing
**A/B Test Template:**
