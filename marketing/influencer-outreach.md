## Outreach Best Practices

### Research Before Sending
- Read their last 5 posts/videos
- Note specific topics they care about
- Check if they've covered similar topics
- Look for mutual connections

### Personalization Checklist
- [ ] Use their actual name (check spelling!)
- [ ] Reference specific content they created
- [ ] Match their tone (formal vs casual)
- [ ] Mention mutual connections if applicable
- [ ] Show you understand their audience

### Follow-Up Strategy
- **Day 0:** Send initial outreach
- **Day 5-7:** Send first follow-up (brief, value-add)
- **Day 14:** Send final follow-up (last chance, no pressure)
- **After Day 14:** Move to "nurture" list (engage with their content, try again in 30 days)

### Tracking Metrics
- Open rate (if using email tracking)
- Response rate
- Positive vs negative responses
- Conversion to call/meeting
- Conversion to partnership/promotion

### Red Flags to Avoid
- Generic "Dear Influencer" emails
- Asking for promotion without offering value
- Being pushy or aggressive
- Not respecting "no" or silence
- Ignoring their content/audience fit
