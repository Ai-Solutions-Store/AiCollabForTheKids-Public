## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Nursing organizations are the voice and infrastructure of the nursing profession. With 4-5 million nurses in the United States represented across dozens of professional organizations, partnership with key nursing bodies provides:

1. **Credibility** - Professional endorsement validates mission and approach
2. **Scale** - Direct access to millions of nurses through organization channels
3. **Community** - Integration into existing nurse communities and networks
4. **Sustainability** - Long-term member benefit programs driving recurring revenue
5. **Impact** - Structured giving mechanisms reaching pediatric charities

This document details the 30+ priority nursing organizations for partnership.
