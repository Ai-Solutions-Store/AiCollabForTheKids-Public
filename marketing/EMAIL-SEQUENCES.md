## 5. SUBJECT LINE A/B TESTING FRAMEWORK

### Testing Methodology
- Test 2 subject lines per campaign (50/50 split)
- Minimum sample size: 1,000 recipients per variant
- Track: Open rate, click rate, conversion rate
- Winner = highest conversion rate (not just opens)

### High-Performing Subject Line Patterns

**PATTERN 1: TRANSPARENCY + NUMBERS**
- "60% of our profits go to Verified Pediatric Charities"
- "How thousands kids got help through an AI platform"
- "The smart contract that guarantees 60/30/10"

**PATTERN 2: MISSION + URGENCY**
- "Your trial ends in 3 days - Don't lose access FOR THE KIDS"
- "50% off to come back (final offer)"
- "Help us reach 5,000 backers by Q1 2026"

**PATTERN 3: CURIOSITY + VALUE**
- "Why a recycling business owner built this AI platform"
- "7 ways to use Team Claude (and help kids while you work)"
- "We built 7 new features since you left"

**PATTERN 4: PERSONAL + DIRECT**
- "We miss you. The kids miss your impact."
- "Thank you. You just helped kids get life-saving care."
- "Your first referral just went Pro. Thank you."

**PATTERN 5: TECHNICAL + Transparent**
- "No treasury. No promises. Just code."
- "The blockchain proof: 60% to Verified Pediatric Charities"
- "How we moved from 50% to 60% (Ethics Override V1.3)"

### Subject Lines to AVOID (Gospel Violations)
- "contribute to help kids" ? Use "Back the mission"
- " giving" ? Never mention taxes
- "Charity platform" ? Use "Profit allocation platform"
- "Your contribution receipt" ? Use "Your purchase receipt"

## 6. OPTIMAL SEND TIMES

### Best Times (Based on Developer/Tech Audience)

**WELCOME EMAILS**
- Send: Immediately upon trigger event
- No delay. Strike while intent is hot.

**EDUCATIONAL EMAILS**
- Best: Tuesday - Thursday, 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM (recipient's timezone)
- Avoid: Monday mornings (inbox overload), Friday afternoons (weekend mode)

**PROMOTIONAL EMAILS (UPGRADES, REFERRALS)**
- Best: Wednesday 11:00 AM or Thursday 1:00 PM
- Avoid: Weekends (lower engagement for B2B/tech audience)

**RE-ENGAGEMENT EMAILS**
- Best: Tuesday 9:00 AM (start of workweek momentum)
- Avoid: Holidays, end of month (busy periods)

**IMPACT UPDATES / REPORTS**
- Best: First Tuesday of the month, 10:00 AM
- Creates "monthly impact check-in" habit

### Timezone Handling
- Segment by timezone (EST, CST, MST, PST, UTC)
- Send emails at 10:00 AM local time for each segment
- Use Mailchimp/SendGrid timezone features

### Frequency Caps
- Maximum 2 emails per week (excluding transactional)
- Minimum 3 days between marketing emails
- Allow users to set email preferences

## 7. A/B TESTING FRAMEWORK

### What to Test

**SUBJECT LINES (Primary Test)**
- Test every campaign
- 50/50 split, minimum 1,000 recipients per variant
- Winner = highest conversion rate (not opens)

**EMAIL BODY (Secondary Test)**
- Test CTA placement (top vs. bottom)
- Test message length (short vs. detailed)
- Test tone (technical vs. emotional)

**CALLS-TO-ACTION (Tertiary Test)**
- Button text: "Go Pro FOR THE KIDS" vs. "Upgrade Now"
- Link color: Coral (#CC785C) vs. Blue (#078EFA)
- CTA count: Single CTA vs. multiple CTAs

### Testing Schedule

**MONTH 1-2: BASELINE**
- Run all sequences as written
- Collect baseline metrics (open rate, click rate, conversion rate)
- No testing yet - just gather data

**MONTH 3-4: SUBJECT LINE TESTS**
- Test 2 subject lines per sequence
- Use A/B testing framework above
- Document winners in shared spreadsheet

**MONTH 5-6: BODY CONTENT TESTS**
- Test CTA placement, message length, tone
- Keep winning subject lines from Month 3-4
- Optimize for conversion rate

**MONTH 7+: CONTINUOUS OPTIMIZATION**
- Run ongoing tests on new campaigns
- Refresh underperforming emails
- Seasonal variations (holiday messaging, Q4 giving season)

### Success Metrics

**WELCOME SEQUENCE**
- Goal: 30%+ open rate, 5%+ click rate, 15%+ conversion to Pro

**BACKER THANK YOU SEQUENCE**
- Goal: 60%+ open rate (they just purchased), 10%+ click rate, 20%+ referral link clicks

**RE-ENGAGEMENT SEQUENCE**
- Goal: 20%+ open rate (dormant users), 8%+ click rate, 10%+ reactivation rate

**REFERRAL REQUEST SEQUENCE**
- Goal: 35%+ open rate, 15%+ referral link clicks, 5%+ successful referrals

## 8. EMAIL SERVICE PROVIDER SETUP

### Recommended ESP: SendGrid (Twilio)

**Why SendGrid:**
- Transactional + Marketing emails in one platform
- API-first (integrates with existing backend)
- Deliverability reputation management
- Timezone scheduling built-in
- A/B testing native support

**API Integration:**
```javascript
// api/routes/email-sequences.js
const sgMail = require('@sendgrid/mail');
sgMail.setApiKey(process.env.SENDGRID_API_KEY);

async function sendWelcomeEmail(user) {
  const msg = {
    to: user.email,
    from: 'hello@aidoesitall.website',
    subject: 'Welcome to the AI that works FOR THE KIDS',
    templateId: 'd-welcome-sequence-01',
    dynamicTemplateData: {
      firstName: user.firstName,
      referralCode: user.referralCode,
    },
  };
  await sgMail.send(msg);
}
```

### Alternative: Mailchimp (If Preferred)
- Better for non-technical marketers
- Drag-and-drop email builder
- Audience segmentation built-in
- Slightly lower deliverability than SendGrid
