## DM SENDING BEST PRACTICES

### Timing
- **Best times:** 9-11 AM or 6-8 PM EST (when creators check DMs)
- **Avoid:** Weekends (lower response rates)
- **Follow-up:** Wait 4-7 days before gentle follow-up

### Platform-Specific Tips

**Twitter/X DMs:**
- Keep under 280 characters for visibility
- Use their @ handle in message
- Include link in SECOND message (avoids spam filter)

**Instagram DMs:**
- Open with emoji for visual break
- Reference their story/recent post
- Keep under 150 words

**LinkedIn Messages:**
- More formal tone
- Lead with mutual connection or shared interest
- 2-3 sentence max

**YouTube Comments (for DM request):**
- Comment on recent video: "Love this! Sent you a collab idea via email/Twitter"
- Drives them to check DMs
