### Response Framework: CLARA Method

**C - Connect** (Empathize with concern)
> "I totally understand that concern - you're not alone in thinking that."

**L - Learn** (Ask clarifying question)
> "Help me understand - what specifically worries you about [objection]?"

**A - Answer** (Address with data + emotion)
> "Here's how we handle that: [specific solution] + [supporting data]"

**R - Reframe** (Shift perspective)
> "Think about it this way: [alternative framing that highlights value]"

**A - Ask** (Trial close)
> "Does that address your concern? Would you be willing to try it for one month?"
