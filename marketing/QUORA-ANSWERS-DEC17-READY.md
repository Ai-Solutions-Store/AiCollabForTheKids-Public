**Step 1: Check Financial Transparency**

Visit the charity's website and look for:
- IRS Form 990 (public tax return showing all income/expenses)
- Audited financial statements from a third-party CPA firm
- Breakdown of how every dollar is spent (program vs. overhead)

Red flag: If they won't publish financials, walk away.

**Step 2: Use Third-Party Rating Sites**

- **Charity Navigator:** Rates financial health and accountability (0-4 stars)
