- Video testimonial case studies
- Written case studies (1,500 words)
- Partner/beneficiary stories

- Detailed success stories
- Partnership testimonials

**Time to Impact:** 16+ weeks

## COMPLIANCE & LEGAL CHECKLIST

**Before Publishing Any Testimonial:**
- [ ] Customer permission obtained (written)
- [ ] Permission to use name/company/photo
- [ ] Permission to use specific metrics/results
- [ ] No misleading claims
- [ ] Results are representative (not exceptional)
- [ ] Proper disclaimer ("results may vary")
- [ ] GDPR compliant (if EU customers)
- [ ] CAN-SPAM compliant (if email)
- [ ] FTC disclosure (if affiliate relationship)
- [ ] Review legal terms with company counsel

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### Email Outreach
❌ Generic, spammy subject lines
❌ Too many asks at once
❌ Too long (should be scannable)
❌ No incentive mentioned
❌ Hard-to-follow CTA
❌ Asking for permission after publishing

✅ Personalized, specific subject
✅ One clear ask
✅ 3-4 paragraph max
✅ Clear incentive
✅ Single prominent CTA link
✅ Get written permission first

---

### Video Testimonials
❌ Poor audio quality
❌ Overly scripted (sounds fake)
❌ Too long for platform
❌ No captions
❌ Harsh lighting
❌ Distracting background

✅ Clear, tested audio
✅ Natural, conversational tone
✅ Platform-specific length
✅ Full captions burned in
✅ Professional, flattering lighting
✅ Clean, intentional background

---

### Case Studies
❌ No real data/metrics
❌ Focuses on FOR THE KIDS, not customer
❌ Too much jargon
❌ Unverifiable claims
❌ Generic story (could apply to anyone)
❌ No clear problem/solution/result

✅ Specific numbers and results
✅ Customer is the hero
✅ Clear, accessible language
✅ Facts verified by customer
✅ Specific story (unique to this customer)
✅ Clear narrative arc

---

### Review Site Management
❌ Asking for 5-star reviews only
❌ Refusing to respond to negative reviews
❌ Fake reviews or incentives inappropriate
❌ Ignoring reviews
❌ Not tracking competitor reviews

✅ Asking for honest reviews
✅ Responding to all reviews (positive + negative)
✅ Appropriate incentives
✅ Regular engagement
✅ Competitive awareness
