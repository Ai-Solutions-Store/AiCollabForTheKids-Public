### SOCIAL MEDIA ENGAGEMENT

**Before Pitching:**
1. Follow podcast on Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn
2. Comment on 2-3 recent episodes (genuine engagement)
3. Share an episode quote with tag
4. THEN send email pitch (they'll recognize your name)

**After Pitching:**
- Continue engaging with content
- Don't spam or pressure
- Let the work speak for itself

### RESPONSE HANDLING

**If They Say Yes:**
- Respond within 2 hours
- Offer 3 date/time options
- Send one-sheet (create if needed)
- Confirm tech setup (Zoom, Riverside, etc.)

**If They Say Not Right Now:**
- Thank them
- Ask if you can follow up in 3-6 months
- Stay engaged with their content

**If They Ghost:**
- Move on after Day 12 email
- Re-engage in 6 months with update ("We just hit $500K contributed...")

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

**Don't Pitch If:**
- Podcast hasn't published in 6+ months (likely defunct)
- Host explicitly says "no pitches" in bio
- Audience is clearly misaligned (e.g., crypto skeptics)
- Recent episode covered similar guest (wait 3-6 months)

**Don't Say:**
- "This will go viral" (cringe)
- "You'd be crazy not to book me" (arrogant)
- "I have a book to promote" (unless they ask)
- "Please support" (we're not a charity)

**Don't Do:**
- Mass BCC emails (hosts can tell)
- Copy-paste without customization
- Pitch via DM before email (unprofessional)
- Follow up daily (annoying)
