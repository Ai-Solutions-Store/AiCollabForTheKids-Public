### DON'T

- Hide business model
- Use emotional appeals

## COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: Should I post all 5 milestones at once?**
A: No. Spread over 12 months. Build narrative momentum.

**Q: What if revenue is lower than templates?**
A: Adjust numbers, keep commitment. Builders respect honesty.

**Q: How do I handle criticism?**
A: Take seriously. Provide proof. Invite verification. No defensiveness.

**Q: Should I disclose Claude wrote these?**
A: No. You're using a tool (like using Stripe). Voice should be yours.

**Q: When should I start?**
A: ASAP. Sooner you build credibility, sooner you get network effects.

## EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

**Post Gets Downvoted:**
- Don't panic. Review feedback. Respond honestly. Learn.

**Smart Contract Vulnerability Found:**
- Respond Tier 1. Take seriously. Offer bounty. Get it fixed. Report back.

**You Miss a Posting Deadline:**
- Post late. Explain briefly. Proceed normally. Don't dwell.

**Revenue Lower Than Expected:**
- Adjust metrics in post. Explain why honestly. Keep commitment.
