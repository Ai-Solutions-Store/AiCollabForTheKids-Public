## Campaign Settings Recommendations

### Budget & Bidding
- **Daily Budget:** Start with $50-100/day
- **Bidding Strategy:** Maximize Conversions (with target CPA after 30 conversions)
- **Target CPA:** $15-25 (adjust based on donation value)

### Targeting
- **Geographic:** United States (expand to UK, Canada, Australia later)
- **Device:** All devices (mobile-optimized landing pages required)
- **Schedule:** 24/7 (peak hours: 6-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 6-10 PM EST)

- **Sitelink Extensions:**

## A/B Testing Plan

### Test 1: Headline Emphasis (Weeks 1-2)
- Variant A: Lead with "60% to Kids"
- Variant B: Lead with "AI-Powered" or "Blockchain"
- **Hypothesis:** Concrete percentage converts better than technology buzzwords

### Test 2: Description CTA (Weeks 3-4)
- Variant A: "Join the movement"
- Variant B: "Start giving today"
- **Hypothesis:** Action-oriented CTAs outperform aspirational language

### Test 3: Display URL Path (Weeks 5-6)
- Variant A: /donate
- Variant B: /give
- Variant C: /impact
- **Hypothesis:** Impact-focused URLs may increase click-through rate

## Quality Score Optimization

### Landing Page Requirements
1. **Speed:** < 2 second load time
2. **Mobile:** Fully responsive, easy donation flow
3. **Message Match:** Headline on ad = headline on landing page
4. **Clear CTA:** Donate button above the fold
5. **Trust Signals:** Display charity verification, blockchain proof, testimonials
