**New AI Platform Commits 60% of Revenue to Children's Hospitals Through Immutable Smart Contract**

*Single Father Builds "Anti-Unicorn" Tech Company Where Charity Comes First - And Can Never Be Changed*

**UNITED STATES - December 17, 2025** - A new AI automation platform has launched with an unprecedented business model: 60% of all profits are mathematically allocated to verified pediatric charities through blockchain smart contracts - and this split can never be altered by investors, board members, or even the founder himself.

FOR THE KIDS (https://aidoesitall.website) is the creation of Joshua Coleman, a single father and technologist who built an AI-powered platform that generates commercial value while structurally allocating the majority of profits to children's hospitals.

**The Immutable Mission**

Unlike traditional companies where charitable giving is discretionary, FOR THE KIDS hardcodes its 60/30/10 revenue split directly into smart contract logic deployed on Base blockchain:

- **60%** → Verified Pediatric Charities
- **30%** → Infrastructure & Platform Growth
- **10%** → Founder Compensation

"I intentionally gave up control," Coleman explains. "The 60% allocation is written into the smart contract. Even if I wanted to change it - which I don't - I mathematically cannot. The code is the contract."

The platform's smart contract (planned for Q1 2026 deployment, with backend enforcement currently active) provides transparent, immutable proof of every allocation. No human intervention can pause, redirect, or reduce the 60% flowing to pediatric care.

**AI Board of Directors**

FOR THE KIDS is governed by what Coleman calls "The AI Board of Directors" - a federated governance model using five AI systems:

- **Claude (Anthropic)** - The Architect: Generates all code and maintains architectural integrity
- **Jules/Gemini (Google)** - The Integrator: Handles business logic validation
- **Grok (xAI)** - The Engineer: Manages infrastructure and deployment
- **Perplexity** - The Researcher: Fact-checking and verification
- **Joshua Coleman** - The Executive: Final mission direction and human oversight

All code modifications are performed exclusively by Claude AI instances, ensuring consistency with the platform's "Gospel" (its immutable governing principles). Every git commit includes: `Co-Authored-By: Claude <noreply@anthropic.com>`.

"Claude doesn't forget the mission, doesn't get tired, and doesn't compromise on the 60%," Coleman says. "Human developers can be pressured. Code cannot."

**The Ethics Override**

In December 2024, Coleman made a decision that shocked industry observers: he voluntarily reduced his own compensation from 20% to 10%, redirecting an additional 10% to children's causes. This "Gospel V1.3 Ethics Override" exemplifies his commitment to putting kids first.

"The founder took less so kids could get more," Coleman says simply. "That's the entire philosophy of this platform."

**Child Safety First**

As a platform explicitly "FOR THE KIDS," child safety is the highest priority:

- COPPA compliant (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- FOSTA/SESTA compliant dating features
- Age-gated AI models (Baby Grok for children, adult models require verification)
- Default assumption: Users are children until proven otherwise
- Zero data collection from minors without parental consent

"If any feature compromises child safety, we delete it immediately," Coleman states. "No exceptions. No negotiations."

**The Anti-Unicorn Business Model**

While venture-backed startups chase billion-dollar valuations, FOR THE KIDS represents what Coleman calls "the anti-unicorn approach":

- No dilution of the 60% allocation through investment rounds
- No pressure to "scale fast and break things"
- No pivot away from the charitable mission when profits grow
- Infrastructure designed for perpetual operation, not exponential growth

"We're not trying to be a unicorn," Coleman says. "We're trying to be eternal. Slow growth, infinite timeline, 60% to kids forever."

**Technical Infrastructure**

The platform runs on a private cluster of recycled enterprise servers:
- 184GB combined RAM across four nodes
- T5500 (192.168.0.101): Docker orchestration, PostgreSQL, Redis
- Sabertooth: Primary development and deployment command center
- 9020: Database replica and failover node
- i3 Sentry: Monitoring and health check automation

"We don't rent cloud servers for vanity metrics," Coleman explains. "Every dollar saved on infrastructure is a dollar that can flow to the kids."

FOR THE KIDS is a commercial AI automation platform where 60% of all profits are mathematically allocated to verified pediatric charities through smart contract logic. The platform is governed by an AI Board of Directors and operates under "The Gospel" - an immutable set of principles that cannot be altered by investors, partners, or market pressure. The mission is eternal.
