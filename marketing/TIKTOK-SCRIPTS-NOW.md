## PRODUCTION NOTES

### EQUIPMENT NEEDED
- Smartphone (4K capable preferred)
- Ring light or natural window light
- Tripod or stable surface
- Wireless mic (optional but recommended)

### FILMING TIPS
1. **Natural light is your friend** - Film near windows during golden hour
2. **Eye level or slightly above** - Most flattering angle
3. **Clean background** - Minimize distractions, or use PC setup as backdrop
4. **Vertical format** - 9:16 aspect ratio ALWAYS
5. **Captions are mandatory** - 85% watch without sound

### EDITING APPS (MOBILE)
- **CapCut** (free, excellent for text overlays)
- **InShot** (quick cuts and music)
- **VN Video Editor** (advanced features)

### POSTING STRATEGY
- **Best times:** 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-11 PM (user's timezone)
- **Cross-post everywhere:** TikTok â†’ Reels â†’ YouTube Shorts â†’ Pinterest
- **Pin first comment** with Kickstarter link + "LINK IN BIO ðŸ‘†"
- **Respond to ALL comments** in first hour (algorithm boost)

### CALL-TO-ACTION VARIATIONS
Use these in rotation to avoid repetition:
- "Link in bio"
- "Kickstarter link below"
- "Bio link - back us now"
- "Swipe up" (Stories)
- "Tap the link in my bio"
