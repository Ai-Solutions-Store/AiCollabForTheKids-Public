### PROGRAM NAME
**"New Policy, New Help" contribution Program**

### OVERVIEW
Insurance carriers partner with FOR THE KIDS to automatically allocate a portion of new policy revenues to pediatric charities.

**Step 1: Carrier Integration**
- Carrier sends customer list of new policyholders (HIPAA-compliant, encrypted)
- FOR THE KIDS creates matching customer profiles in platform
- New customer receives welcome email (carrier-branded)

**Step 2: Customer Welcome Campaign**
- Email subject: "Welcome to [Carrier]. Plus: We're Helping Kids for You."
- Message: "Your new policy protects your family. We're celebrating by funding pediatric charities in your honor."
- CTA: "See how many kids your policy helps" (link to FOR THE KIDS platform)

**Step 3: Automatic Allocation**
- Carrier sends quarterly contribution (e.g., $50-500 per new policy for first year)
- Automatic webhook triggers FOR THE KIDS DAO smart contract
- 100% of carrier contribution goes to verified pediatric charities
- No commission, no overhead

**Step 4: Transparency & Reporting**
- Customer receives: "Your policy generated $XXX for kids' hospitals"
- Carrier receives: "This quarter, we helped XXX kids from our new policyholders"
- Both receive: Blockchain-verified proof of charitable impact
