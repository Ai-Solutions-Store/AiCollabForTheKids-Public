## BEST RESPONSE TIMES

### Response Window Strategy

**HARO Query Lifecycle:**
- Query sent to journalists: 5:00 AM EST
- Journalists have 24 hours to respond
- Queries close at 5:00 PM EST next day
- Best submissions received: 8:00 AM - 2:00 PM EST

### Optimal Response Timing

| Time Window | Response Rate | Quality | Strategy |
|-------------|--------------|---------|----------|
| **0-2 hours** | 35% ? | 9/10 | HIGHEST priority (beat competitors) |
| **2-6 hours** | 25% ? | 8/10 | Still strong (most journalists start here) |
| **6-12 hours** | 20% ? | 8/10 | Good (lunchtime check) |
| **12-18 hours** | 15% | 7/10 | Declining (competition increased) |
| **18-24 hours** | 10% | 6/10 | Last resort (most slots filled) |

### Why Early Responses Win

1. **First-in advantage** - Journalists often use first quality response
2. **Less competition** - Early responders beat late responders 90% of the time
3. **Freshness** - Your quote is fresher in journalist's mind
4. **Selection** - They might stop reading after finding what they need
5. **Authority** - Early responders seem more authoritative ("they respond immediately")

### Daily Response Routine

**Morning (6:30 AM EST):**
```
1. Check email for new HARO queries (5:00-6:30 AM batch)
2. Read subject lines (2 min)
3. Identify top 3-5 best fit queries (3 min)
4. Draft responses using templates (10 min each = 50 min)
5. Customize each response (5 min each = 25 min)
6. Send all by 7:30 AM (30 min before peak response time)
Total time: ~90 minutes ? ~3-5 quality responses
```

**Lunch (11:30 AM EST):**
```
1. Check for missed morning queries
2. Respond to any high-fit queries from 8 AM batch
3. Send 1-2 afternoon responses (keep them fresh)
```

**Evening (5:00 PM EST):**
```
1. Check last remaining open queries
2. Only respond if PERFECT fit (don't waste bad responses)
3. Review what queries came in today
4. Plan tomorrow's priority list
```

### Query Priority Scoring

Rate each query on 1-5 scale:

| Factor | Points | Scoring |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Alignment | 0-5 | Does it fit our story? (5=perfect, 0=no fit) |
| Audience | 0-3 | Size/quality of publication (3=major outlet) |
| Timing | 0-2 | Response time to close (2=early, 0=deadline) |
| Effort | 0-2 | Can use template? (2=easy, 0=custom needed) |
| **TOTAL** | **0-12** | **Score** |

**Response Rules:**
- Score 10-12: Respond immediately (use template, customize)
- Score 8-9: Respond within 2 hours
- Score 6-7: Respond within 6 hours
- Score 4-5: Respond if time available
- Score 0-3: Skip (save time for better queries)
