## PRODUCTION SPECIFICATIONS

### Video Format Requirements
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 pixels (vertical, 9:16 aspect ratio)
- **Frame Rate:** 24-30 fps (TikTok/Instagram optimized)
- **Codec:** H.264 (universal compatibility)
- **File Size:** Under 650 MB (TikTok limit)

### Text Overlay Standards
- **Font:** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins, or Impact)
- **Size:** 60-80pt (readable on mobile screens)
- **Color:** High contrast (white text on dark, dark text on light)
- **Duration:** 2-3 seconds per text overlay minimum
- **Position:** Center screen, avoid corners (TikTok UI elements)

### Audio Requirements
- **Trending Sounds:** Source from TikTok/Instagram built-in libraries (royalty-free)
- **Alternative Sounds:** Epidemic Sound ($15/mo), Artlist ($20/mo)
- **Avoid:** Copyrighted music without license
- **Loudness:** -14 LUFS (TikTok optimization)

### Captions/Accessibility
- **Auto-Captions:** Enable (TikTok/Instagram built-in)
- **Manual Captions:** Add to YouTube Shorts
- **Color Contrast:** WCAG AA minimum compliance
- **No Flickering:** Avoid flashing text (photosensitivity safety)
