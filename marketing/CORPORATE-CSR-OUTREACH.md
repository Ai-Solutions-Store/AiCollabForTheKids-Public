## PART 2: CORPORATE MATCHING GIFT PROGRAM INTEGRATION

### WHAT IS A MATCHING GIFT PROGRAM?

Corporate matching gift programs allow employees to:
1. Make a personal contribution to a nonprofit
2. Submit the contribution receipt to employer's HR/benefits portal
3. Employer "matches" the gift (typically 1:1 or 2:1)

**For example:** Employee contributes $500 → Company matches → Nonprofit receives $1,000
