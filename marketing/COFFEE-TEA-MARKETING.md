## 1. COFFEE SHOP ROUND-UP PROGRAM

### Overview
Partner with independent and chain coffee shops to implement a digital round-up system at checkout. Customers can round up their purchase to the nearest dollar or contribute a fixed amount ($1, $5, $10) with every transaction.

### Implementation Strategy

**Phase 1: Deck Development (Week 1)**
- Create partner pitch deck emphasizing brand enhancement
- Include: customer engagement data, charity impact stories, POS integration details
- Design: Apple Pay/Google Pay compatible checkout flow
- Add white-label option for shop branding

**Phase 2: Target Coffee Shop Partnerships**

**Independent Cafes (High-Impact):**
- Coffee shops in affluent neighborhoods (stronger contribution capacity)
- Third-wave specialty coffee shops (mission-aligned audiences)
- Cafe chains with 5-25 locations (decision speed advantage)
- Coffee shops with active social media followings (amplification)

**Chain Partnerships:**
- Peet's Coffee (West Coast, premium positioning)
- Intelligentsia Coffee (specialty focus, urban locations)
- Blue Bottle Coffee (brand prestige)
- Local roaster chains (5-10 shop networks)

### Key Messaging
```
"Let Your Coffee Support Children"
- Easy checkout integration
- Real-time impact tracking
- Customer goodwill & retention
- Social proof for your shop
- 60% directly to verified pediatric charities
```

### Partner Benefits
- White-labeled dashboard showing shop's collective contributions
- Monthly impact report with charity stories
- Social media content (customer testimonials, impact updates)
- Co-branded signage & cup sleeves
- Tax documentation for business contributions

### Operational Details

**POS Integration:**
- Stripe integration for payment processing
- Square integration for shops using Square
- Manual tablet option for shops with older systems
- Real-time transaction tracking

**Technology:**
- Dedicated landing page: aidoesitall.website/round-up
- QR codes for storefronts linking to contribution portal
- SMS receipts with impact messaging
- Monthly email digests to participating shops

**Launch Goals:**
- Target: 250 coffee shops in Year 1
- Focus: Major metro areas (NYC, LA, SF, Chicago, Seattle, Austin, Boston)

## 3. COFFEE ROASTER CHARITY BLEND

### Campaign: "Hearts & Beans" Signature Charity Blend

### Concept
Partner with premium coffee roasters to create a limited-edition "Hearts & Beans" blend exclusively sold through aidoesitall.website. 60% of profits go directly to pediatric charities.

### Roaster Partnership Targets

**Tier 1: Premium Roasters (3-5 partners)**
- **Intelligentsia Coffee** (LA-based, premium positioning)
- **Blue Bottle Coffee** (Tokyo-based, premium specialty)
- **Counter Culture Coffee** (Durham, NC - ethical sourcing focus)
- **Onyx Coffee Labs** (Arkansas - specialty community)
- **Dripkit/Dripper** (Emerging specialty, sustainable practices)

**Tier 2: Regional Roasters (10-15 partners)**
- Local/regional roasters in coffee hubs
- Criteria: Ethical sourcing, community focus, quality reputation

### Blend Development Process

**Phase 1: Collaboration**
- Co-develop blend with roaster's head roaster
- Test roast: 3-5 iterations
- Finalize flavor profile: fruity, balanced, approachable
- Branding: "Hearts & Beans" artwork (design details below)

**Phase 2: Sourcing**
- Single-origin or blend composition
- Transparency: Include origin cards showing farm stories
- Sustainability: FairTrade/Direct Trade preferred
- Volume: 500-1,000 lbs per roaster per run

**Phase 3: Packaging**
- Custom bag design: "Hearts & Beans" + charity messaging
- Bag sizes: 12oz (standard), 5lb (bulk), 1-cup pour-overs
- QR code linking to: Charity story + tasting notes + brewing guide
- Sustainability: Compostable bags preferred

### Distribution Strategy

**Primary Channel:** aidoesitall.website/coffee-blends
- Direct DTC sales
- Subscription option: Monthly shipment
- Bundle deals: 2 bags + merchandise

**Secondary Channels:**
- Partner roaster websites (affiliate link to our site)
- Amazon (Fulfilled by Amazon)
- Specialty coffee retailers (partnered locations)
- Cafe partnerships (private label option)

### Pricing & Economics

**Wholesale Cost (from roaster):** $4.50-6.00 per 12oz bag
**Retail Price:** $15.99 per 12oz bag
**Gross Revenue per Bag:** $15.99
**Cost of Goods:** $6.00
**Packaging/Shipping:** $2.00
**Payment Processing:** $1.28
**Gross Profit:** $6.71 per bag

**Charity Distribution (60%):**
- $6.71 × 60% = $4.03 per bag → Verified pediatric charities
- $6.71 × 30% = $2.01 per bag → Infrastructure/platform
- $6.71 × 10% = $0.67 per bag → Founder

**Sales Projections Year 1:**
- Conservative: 2,000 bags = $13,420 to charities
- Moderate: 5,000 bags = $33,550 to charities
- Optimistic: 10,000 bags = $67,100 to charities
