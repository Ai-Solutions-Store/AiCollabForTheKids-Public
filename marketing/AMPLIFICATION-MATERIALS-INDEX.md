## SUCCESS METRICS

### By Document

**Social Media Guidelines:**
- % of advocates reading it (target: 95% by end of Week 2)
- Compliance violations (target: <5% of posts)
- Engagement rates (average likes/comments on advocate posts)
- Network growth (followers gained by advocates)

**Ready-to-Share Templates:**
- % of advocates using templates (target: 70%)
- Template usage frequency (avg templates per person per month)
- Performance of templated posts (engagement benchmark)
- Customization depth (% adding personal flavor vs. copy-paste)

**Incentive Program:**
- Point velocity (avg points earned per advocate per month)
- Engagement levels (% actively earning points)
- Tier advancement (% moving up tiers quarterly)
- Reward redemption (% of points redeemed)
- Retention (3-month, 6-month, 12-month activity levels)

**Training Materials:**
- % completing foundation modules (target: 90%)
- Avg modules per person (target: 5+ over 6 months)
- Post-training activity increase (shares, referrals)
- Knowledge retention (quiz scores)
- Long-term impact (tier advancement of trained advocates)

### Aggregate Impact
```
REACH METRICS:
- Total shares per month (all advocates combined)
- Total impressions (estimated from platform data)
- Total referral traffic (clicks from advocate links)
- New customers from advocacy (attribution)

IMPACT METRICS:
- Revenue attributed to advocacy ($)
- Children helped ($ × 60% ÷ $2,000)
- New advocates recruited (multiplication effect)
- Community size (total active advocates)

QUALITY METRICS:
- Content authenticity scores (peer rating)
- Gospel compliance rate (Gospel accuracy)
- Audience engagement quality (comments vs. likes)
- Sustainability (long-term engagement vs. burnout)
```

## FINAL NOTES

### Philosophy Behind These Materials

**Clarity over Complexity**
Each document is designed to be accessible. No corporate jargon. Clear structure. Easy to navigate.

**Flexibility over Rigidity**
Guidelines are principles, not rules. Templates are starting points, not requirements. Training is a menu, not a mandatory path.

**Authenticity over Perfection**
These materials emphasize being yourself, finding your voice, and sharing naturally—not becoming a corporate spokesperson.

**Scalability over Boutique**
Designed to work for 10 advocates or 1,000. Systems scale. Humans scale. Community scales.

### Long-Term Vision

These four documents form the foundation of a **sustainable, scalable advocacy program** that:
- Creates clear pathways (from new to experienced to leader)
- Removes barriers to participation (templates, training, incentives)
- Maintains compliance (Gospel V1.3 built into everything)
- Celebrates authenticity (not forcing corporate speak)
- Builds community (recognition, peer connection, shared mission)

The goal: **Make advocacy easy, rewarding, and meaningful.**
