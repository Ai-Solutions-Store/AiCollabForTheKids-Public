## KEY PRINCIPLES

### Lead with TECHNOLOGY
- Smart contract address
- Real metrics (numbers, not vibes)
- Specific mechanisms (how it works)
- Links to verification (audit, Basescan)

### AVOID
- Charity language ("helping kids")
- Preachy tone
- Non-profit positioning (it's for-profit!)
- Vague promises
- Hype words

### TONE REQUIREMENTS
- Specific, not vague
- Technical, not emotional
- Humble, admit unknowns
- Action-oriented, show execution
- Non-defensive, welcome criticism

## EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

**Post Gets Downvoted?**
- Don't panic
- Review feedback
- Respond honestly
- Ask follow-up questions
- Learn for next post

**Smart Contract Issue Found?**
- Respond Tier 1 immediately
- Take seriously
- Offer bounty/credit
- Fix it
- Report back publicly

**Revenue Lower Than Expected?**
- Adjust post metrics to reality
- Explain why honestly
- Keep 60% allocation real
- Builders respect honesty

**Miss a Deadline?**
- Post late (honesty > schedule)
- Explain briefly why
- Proceed normally
- Don't dwell on it
