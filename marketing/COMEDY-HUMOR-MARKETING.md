# COMEDY & HUMOR MARKETING STRATEGY FOR THE KIDS
## Campaign URL: https://aidoesitall.website
## Mission: 60% of All Revenue to Verified Pediatric Charities

**Tagline:** *Laughter Heals - Help Kids Laugh!*

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This comprehensive comedy and humor marketing strategy leverages the universal power of laughter to drive engagement and fundraising for pediatric charities. By partnering with comedians, comedy influencers, and entertainment platforms, FOR THE KIDS will create authentic, hilarious content that makes people want to allocate while having fun.

**Key Promise:** Every laugh funds a child's healing. Every share saves a life.
