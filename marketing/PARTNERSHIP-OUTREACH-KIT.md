### Follow-Up Best Practices

**Timing:**
- Wait 5-7 days between emails
- Respect time zones (send 9-11 AM recipient's time)
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)
- Tuesday-Thursday are optimal

**Tone:**
- Professional but personable
- Respectful of their time
- Non-pushy (give easy outs)
- Value-focused (what's in it for them)

**Subject Lines:**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Reference original subject or add "Re:"
- Create curiosity without clickbait
- Be clear about email purpose

**Content:**
- Shorter with each follow-up
- Add new value (don't just repeat)
- Make it easy to respond (yes/no questions)
- Give clear next steps

**When to Stop:**
- After 3-4 emails with no response
- If they explicitly say not interested
- If they ask you to stop
- If you find a better contact

**Long-Term Nurture:**
- Add to newsletter (if they've engaged)
- Connect on LinkedIn (mutual connection)
- Engage with their content (authentic)
- Re-reach out in 6-12 months
