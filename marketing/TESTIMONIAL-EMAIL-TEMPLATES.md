## OVERVIEW
This document contains 8 proven email templates for requesting testimonials from different customer segments. Each template is designed for maximum response rates with minimal friction.

**Key Principles:**
- Personalization (use customer names and specific achievements)
- Value exchange (offer incentives)
- Frictionless process (direct links, 2-minute asks)
- Mission alignment (emphasize the 60% charity impact)
- Clear CTAs (one primary action per email)

## TEMPLATE CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

### Personalization Variables

Replace these with actual customer data:

- [CUSTOMER NAME] → First name or full name
- [COMPANY NAME] → Customer's company
- [SPECIFIC METRIC] → Their actual achievement (revenue, time saved, customers served)
- [TIMEFRAME] → Their tenure (30 days, 6 months, 1 year)
- [SPECIFIC RESULT] → Quantifiable outcome
- [RELEVANT PROBLEM] → Their industry problem
- [DATE] → Specific calendar date
- [AMOUNT] → Charity contribution amount
- [FORM LINK] → Your actual Typeform/Google Form link
- [CALENDAR LINK] → Your Calendly or scheduling link
- [UPLOAD LINK] → Video upload destination (Google Drive, Vimeo, etc.)

### Tone Variations

**Option 1: Friendly & Casual**
- Use contractions (we're, don't)
- Shorter paragraphs
- Emojis in key points
- Conversational language

**Option 2: Professional & Formal**
- Full spelling (we are, do not)
- Longer paragraphs
- No emojis
- Industry terminology

**Option 3: Mission-Focused**
- Emphasize the 60% charity model
- Lead with impact
- Connect to beneficiaries
- Make the "why" clear

Choose tone based on customer segment and brand voice.
