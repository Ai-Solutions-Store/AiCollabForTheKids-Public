## Campaign Overview

The FOR THE KIDS Photography Community Marketing Campaign mobilizes photographers, creators, and visual storytellers to support verified pediatric charities. By leveraging the universal power of photography to tell stories and evoke emotion, we create multiple revenue streams while strengthening photographer communities and building brand loyalty.

**Revenue Split (Gospel V1.3):**
- 60% → Verified Pediatric Charities
- 30% → Infrastructure & Operations
- 10% → Founder/Platform Growth

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How much of my money goes to charities?**
A: 60% of all FOR THE KIDS revenue goes to verified pediatric charities. 30% funds infrastructure, 10% supports founder/growth.

**Q: Which charities do you support?**
A: We partner with St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, Make-A-Wish, Children's Miracle Network, Pediatric Cancer Research Foundation, and others. Photographers can often choose which verified charity receives their session revenue.

**Q: How do I sign up as a photographer?**
A: Visit https://aidoesitall.website and click "Join as Photographer." Complete profile, set your pricing tier, and start getting matched with sessions.

**Q: Is this really free for patients/families?**
A: Click for Kids events are completely free. Charity portrait sessions are discounted (families pay 40% of normal session fee, we cover 60%).

**Q: Can I submit multiple times to the photo contest?**
A: Yes, photographers can submit multiple entries. Each submission is judged on its own merit.

**Q: What if I don't have professional photography equipment?**
A: Many programs accept smartphone photography. We also provide equipment loans/rental for photographers in need.

**Q: How is patient privacy protected?**
A: All hospital photography is HIPAA-compliant. Photographers sign confidentiality agreements. Patients/families sign photo release forms. We never share identifying information without permission.
