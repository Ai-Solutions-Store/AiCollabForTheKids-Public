## THE 12 CAMPAIGN PILLARS

### 1. RAVELRY COMMUNITY (2.5M+ Crafters)
- Create FOR THE KIDS store + designer program
- Build active community group (target: 200K members)
- Designer royalty boost: 40% instead of 20%
- Pattern swap + gamification

### 2. KNITTING INFLUENCERS (25 Accounts)
- Tier 1: 3 mega-influencers (1M+ followers) - $5K + 2% revenue share
- Tier 2: 5 established creators (100K-1M) - $1.5K + 1% revenue share
- Tier 3: 17 micro-influencers (10K-100K) - Free + 10% affiliate
- Total Budget: $22.5K Year 1

### 3. YARN SHOP PARTNERSHIPS (5,000+ Shops)
- Platinum (100): $2K min/month, 30% margin + 5% revenue share
- Gold (500): $500 min/month, 30% margin + 3% revenue share
- Silver (2,000+): Any size, 30% margin, directory listing
- In-store: Monthly "Stitch for Kids" workshops, exclusive yarn drops

### 4. SEWING PATTERNS (12 Exclusive)
- Monthly pattern release (Jan-Dec)
- Primary distribution: Etsy, Craftsy, Ravelry
- Price: $5.99-$12.99 per pattern
- 60% to charity immediately
- Year 1 Target: 50K+ downloads, $200K+ revenue

### 5. QUILTING GUILDS (50,000+ Members)
- Guild adoption program (specialty focus: oncology, cardiac, trauma, etc.)
- Each guild produces 1 community quilt per season
- Hospital contributions + co-marketing
- 12 major quilt show booths nationwide
- Year 1 Goal: 50+ guild partnerships, 100+ quilts contributed

### 6. FABRIC STORE PARTNERSHIPS (Similar to Yarn)
- Platinum (50): $5K min/month inventory
- Gold (200): $2K min/month
- Silver (1,000+): Directory listing
- Year 1 Target: $2M+ fabric sales, $1.2M to charity

### 7. KNIT FOR KIDS PROGRAM (Core Engagement)
- Track A: Free community contribution (items contributed to hospitals)
- Track B: Paid fundraiser (crafters sell items, 60% to charity)
- Monthly challenges + Stitch-a-Thon events
- Real-time impact tracker
- Year 1 Goal: 5,000+ knitters, 10,000+ items, 1M+ impressions

### 8. SEWING MACHINE PARTNERSHIPS (Brother, Singer, Bernina)
- Co-branded bundles ($349 retail, $50 to charity per bundle)
- Loyalty program integration (earn 3 points per $1 on patterns)
- "Brother 4KIDS" machine (teal color, hospital-friendly design)
- Year 1 Target: 10,000+ machines, $500K bundle revenue, $250K contributions

### 9. HANDMADE BLANKET CAMPAIGN (Direct Hospital Placement)
- 50 hospital partnerships
- 3-tier maker program (certified, community, guild)
- 30-day placement guarantee (maker to bedside)
- Quality vetting + HIPAA-safe photo feedback
- Year 1 Goal: 5,000+ blankets, our mission to help children

### 10. CRAFT CIRCLES (1,000+ Small Groups)
- 6-15 person weekly meetup groups
- Monthly challenges (themed patterns)
- Digital leaderboard + impact tracking
- Recognition tiers (Bronze/Silver/Gold)
- Year 1 Target: 1,000 circles, 100K crafters, 12,000 items

### 11. EMBROIDERY COMMUNITY (3M+ Enthusiasts)
- 12 exclusive embroidery designs ($7.99-$8.99 each)
- 20 influencer partnerships
- Co-branded embroidery machines (Husqvarna Viking)
- Monthly online meetups + annual symposium
- Year 1 Goal: 50K+ downloads, $300K+ revenue, $180K to charity

### 12. CROCHET CHARITY PATTERNS (4M+ Crocheters, 30% growth)
- 12 monthly patterns ($4.99-$7.99)
- 20 crochet influencers (distinct from knitting)
- TikTok-first strategy (short-form video optimization)
- 3 crochet books (print + ebook)
- Year 1 Target: 100K+ downloads, $500K+ revenue, $300K to charity

## COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES

1. **Mission-First Structure** - 60% goes to kids automatically (not a contribution, it's structural)
2. **Community Owned** - Craft circles, guilds, shops are co-creators, not just customers
3. **All Fiber Arts** - Knit, sew, embroider, crochet, quilt (competitors focus on one)
4. **Authentic Impact** - Real hospitals, real kids, real stories (HIPAA-safe)
5. **Sustainable** - Supports independent shops, reduces fast fashion
6. **Transparent** - Blockchain verification of charity split
7. **Viral Potential** - TikTok-optimized content, influencer-driven, community-powered
