## STRATEGIC RANKING METHODOLOGY

Communities are ranked by:
1. **Audience Alignment** - How well the community matches FOR THE KIDS mission
2. **Member Quality** - Decision-makers vs. tire-kickers
3. **Engagement Potential** - How receptive to our mission-driven model
4. **Traffic Volume** - Potential reach multiplied by conversion likelihood
5. **Barrier to Entry** - How easy to join and start contributing
6. **Gospel Fit** - How naturally the community embraces the 60/30/10 model

- Track metrics carefully

**Tier 2:**
- Post within 7-10 days

**Tier 3:**
- Post after proven success in Tier 1-2
