## RISK MITIGATION

### Potential Challenges & Solutions

| Challenge | Risk | Mitigation |
|-----------|------|-----------|
| Influencer Saturation | Too many influencers, message diluted | Tier approach, quality over quantity |
| Quality Control (contributions) | Poor-quality items rejected by hospitals | Strict vetting, training, certification |
| Community Burnout | Crafters overwhelmed by monthly challenges | Optional participation, varied difficulty |
| Supply Chain Issues | Yarn/fabric shortages impact campaigns | Multiple vendor partnerships, local sourcing |
| Payment Processing | Payment gateway issues | 3+ payment processor redundancy |
| IP/Copyright | Patterns copied/redistributed | DMCA protection, watermarking, licensing |
| Hospital Partnerships | Hospitals unresponsive/unsupportive | Clear agreements, dedicated liaisons |
| Seasonal Fluctuation | Q1/Q3 lower sales | Holiday bundles, gift guides, bulk orders |
