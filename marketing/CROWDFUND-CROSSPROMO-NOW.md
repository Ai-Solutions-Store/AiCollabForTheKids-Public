## OUTREACH BEST PRACTICES

1. **Personalize every message** - reference specific content
2. **Lead with value** - what can you offer them?
3. **Be transparent** - explain your mission clearly
4. **Show you've done homework** - demonstrate knowledge of their work
5. **Offer real budget** - don't ask for free work
6. **Follow up** - but don't be pushy (2-3 touches max)
7. **Support first** - buy their coffee, join their Patreon before reaching out
8. **Respect their time** - be concise and clear

### DON'TS:
1. **Don't mass email** - no generic blasts
2. **Don't lowball** - pay fair rates
3. **Don't over-promise** - be realistic about reach and impact
4. **Don't pressure** - if they're not interested, move on gracefully
5. **Don't ask for free work** - budget for all collaborations
6. **Don't ghost** - if you commit, follow through
7. **Don't be transactional** - build real relationships
