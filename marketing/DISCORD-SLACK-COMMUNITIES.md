## GENERAL PROMOTION GUIDELINES

### DO's:
1. **Lead with Mission:** Always mention the 60% allocation upfront
2. **Be Transparent:** Link to Gospel V1.3 documentation if asked
3. **Show Impact:** Share verified charity partnerships when available
4. **Be Genuine:** Engage authentically before promoting
5. **Respect Rules:** Read server rules - many have self-promotion channels/days
6. **Provide Value:** Share insights, help others, build reputation first
7. **Use Campaign URL:** Always include https://aidoesitall.website
8. **Track Engagement:** Note which communities respond best

### DON'Ts:
1. **Don't Spam:** One thoughtful post > 10 generic blasts
2. **Don't Ghost:** If people engage, respond thoughtfully
3. **Don't Oversell:** Let the mission speak for itself
4. **Don't Ignore Rules:** Instant ban if you break community guidelines
5. **Don't Copy-Paste:** Customize each message for the community
6. **Don't Argue:** If moderated, respect it and move on
7. **Don't Claim Medical Benefits:** We fund charities, not provide medical services
8. **Don't Forget Gospel:** Always say 60%, never 60% (Gospel V1.3 compliance)

### POSTING SCHEDULE:
- **Monday-Wednesday:** Tech/AI communities (high engagement)
- **Wednesday-Friday:** Entrepreneur/startup communities
- **Weekends:** Parenting communities (more leisure time)
- **Anytime:** Charity/healthcare (professional communities check regularly)

### RESPONSE TEMPLATES:

#### When Asked About Verification:
"We partner with verified 501(c)(3) pediatric charities. Our Gospel V1.3 protocol mandates 60% revenue allocation - not 'up to 60%' but EXACTLY 60%. We're building blockchain verification for full transparency."

#### When Asked About the Business Model:
"We provide AI-powered tools and services. 60% of every dollar goes to verified pediatric charities, 30% to infrastructure/scaling, 10% to founder. It's locked in our core protocol."

#### When Asked About Tax Deductions:
"We're a for-profit social enterprise. Your purchase isn't , but 60% of your payment goes directly to 501(c)(3) charities that issue tax receipts to their supporters."

#### When Asked About Which Charities:
"We partner with verified pediatric healthcare charities including children's hospitals, pediatric research organizations, and family support services. All partners are vetted 501(c)(3) organizations."

### ENGAGEMENT METRICS TO TRACK:
- Which communities generate the most clicks to campaign URL
- Which tone/angle resonates best per community type
- Response time (engage within 2 hours for best results)
- Conversion mentions (anyone who says they contributed/subscribed)
- Community feedback (positive/negative sentiment)

### RED FLAGS (Don't Post If):
- Community explicitly bans commercial content
- You haven't read the server rules
- Server is inactive (last post >7 days ago)
- Community is private/invite-only without proper vetting
- You're not willing to engage with responses
