## 3. NURSERY CHARITY PLANT SALE

### Objective
Host charity plant sale events at partner nurseries with 100% of proceeds (or 60%) going to verified pediatric charities.

### Event Format

**"Growing Hearts Charity Plant Sale"**
- 4-week campaign (April-May or September-October - prime gardening seasons)
- Partner with 10-25 local/regional nurseries
- Each event: 1 day per week or weekend

### Plant Selection

**Affordable, Popular Plants ($5-$25)**
- Succulents and air plants (trendy, low maintenance)
- Herbs (practical value)
- Native plants (environmental impact)
- Starter houseplants (spider plants, pothos, snake plants)
- Seedlings (vegetable and flower)

**Premium Offerings ($25-$100)**
- Mature specimen plants
- Rare or specialty plants
- Plant + educational kit bundles

### Event Structure

1. **Pre-Event (2 weeks prior)**
   - Nursery contributes or discounts plant inventory
   - Social media campaign: #GrowingHeartsForKids
   - Email blast to FOR THE KIDS subscribers
   - Local media outreach (see press release below)
   - Influencer partnerships to promote event

2. **Day-Of Event**
   - Branded signage: "60% of today's sales go to [Verified Pediatric Charity]"
   - Educational booth explaining Gospel V1.3
   - Gardening expert demonstrations (10am, 1pm, 3pm)
   - Kids' corner: free plant activity for children (age-appropriate planting)
   - Photo booth: "I'm growing hearts for [Charity Name]" + social media share
   - Music and light refreshments

3. **Post-Event (1 week after)**
   - Thank you social media posts
   - Impact report: "$X raised, Y plants sold, Z kids impacted"
   - supporter tax receipts
   - Impact story: "Here's where your money went"
   - Incentive for next event: early-bird discounts

### Revenue Model

**Funding Source Options:**

Option A: Plant contributions
- Nursery contributes surplus/clearance plants
- 60% of sale price goes to charity
- Nursery benefits: inventory clearance + brand association

Option B: Nursery Partnership + Platform Commission
- Nursery reduces plant prices 20-30%
- Nursery covers costs, keeps 30%, charity gets 60%, platform gets 10%
- FOR THE KIDS promotes heavily

Option C: FOR THE KIDS Buys Inventory
- FOR THE KIDS negotiates wholesale pricing
- Resells at retail: 60% to charity, 30% to operations, 10% marketing

### Event Checklist

- [ ] Partner nursery selected and committed
- [ ] Plant inventory secured (minimum 500 plants)
- [ ] Charity partner confirmed and prepared for announcement
- [ ] Marketing materials designed (posters, social graphics)
- [ ] Social media campaign scheduled (3+ posts per week)
- [ ] Press releases distributed to local media

- [ ] Volunteer team recruited (10-15 people for event day)
