**Platform:** https://aidoesitall.website
**Mission:** 60% to Verified Pediatric Charities | 30% Infrastructure | 10% Founder
**Version:** 1.0 - Gospel V1.3 Compliant

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This gamified referral program transforms the basic referral mechanics into an engaging, competitive experience that increases sharing by 3x. By leveraging points, tiers, badges, leaderboards, and social mechanics, we create a viral loop that accelerates FOR THE KIDS adoption while maintaining our charitable mission.

**Core Principle:** Every element of gamification reinforces that referrals = more kids helped. Sharing isn't transactional; it's mission-driven.

## 1. POINT SYSTEM DESIGN

### Point Earning Mechanics

**Primary Point Sources:**

| Action | Points | Notes | Gospel Impact |
|--------|--------|-------|----------------|
| **New Referral Sign-up** | 100 pts | One-time when friend creates account | Kids: +60% |
| **Referred Friend's First Purchase** | 50 pts | Bonus when referral converts to paying customer | Kids: +60% |
| **Referred Friend's First $100 Revenue** | 100 pts | Milestone bonus (paid monthly) | Kids: +60% |
| **Referred Friend Reaches 3-Month Active** | 150 pts | Retention milestone | Kids: +60% |
| **Social Share (Twitter/LinkedIn)** | 25 pts | Per share, max 5/day | Awareness: +1 |
| **Charity Milestone Reached** | 200 pts | When their referrals reach $1000 cumulative revenue | Kids: +60% |
| **Team Challenge Participation** | 50-300 pts | Varies by challenge | Community: +1 |
| **Leaderboard Top 10** | Daily 10 pts | Daily participation bonus for leaderboard | Engagement: +1 |
| **Refer a Team** | 500 pts | Refer 5+ people as a cohesive group | Community: +1 |
| **Seasonal Event Completion** | 100-500 pts | Varies by event scope | Engagement: +1 |

### Point Conversion Mechanics

**1 Point = $0.01 Platform Credit OR 1 Point toward Tier Progression**

Users choose: **Redeem Points NOW** or **Convert to Tier XP** for higher-tier unlocks

```
Example:
- User earns 1,000 points (100 referrals)
- Option A: Convert to $10 platform credit immediately
- Option B: Convert to 1,000 Tier XP → Faster progression to Silver Tier
- Most competitive users choose Option B (psychological principle: status > money)
```

### Point Decay Policy

- Points never expire while user is active (quarterly login)
- 90-day inactivity: Points frozen (can be reactivated with login)
- Account deletion: Points contributed to platform charity fund
