## SECTION 2: FIRE DEPARTMENT PARTNERSHIP PLAYBOOK

### Partnership Model Overview

**Foundation Concept:**
"Fill the Boot" is America's most beloved first responder fundraiser. FOR THE KIDS modernizes it with technology while honoring tradition.

**Core Partnership Structure:**

```
TRADITIONAL FILL THE BOOT:
- Firefighters collect cash at intersections
- Results: $500-$2,000 per department
- Impact: Unknown (money usually absorbed into general charity fund)
- Participation: 30-60% of departments nationally

FOR THE KIDS FILL THE BOOT:
- Firefighters collect cash at intersections
- QR codes link to FOR THE KIDS platform
- Online giving option (for digital age)
- Real-time impact tracker (shows exactly where money goes)
- Results: 2-3x increase in contributions
- Impact: Transparent (supporters see specific pediatric charities helped)
- Participation goal: 100+ departments (Year 1)
```

---

### Three-Tier Fire Department Partnership

#### TIER 1: "Boot Drive Partner" Departments (Entry Level)

**Commitment Level:** Low
**Time Investment:** 4 hours per department
**Expected contribution:** $1,000-$3,000 per drive

**What Department Does:**
1. Stages boots at 3-5 high-traffic intersections
2. Shares QR codes linking to FOR THE KIDS contribution page
3. Uses provided marketing materials (we print them)
4. Posts 2-3 social media updates during boot drive
5. Reports final totals to FOR THE KIDS team

**What FOR THE KIDS Provides:**
- Custom boot design (FOR THE KIDS branded)
- QR codes (printed, ready to use)
- Social media graphics + copy
- Email template to send community
- Posters (print-ready)
- contribution landing page (custom for department)
- Real-time tracking dashboard
- Post-drive impact report

**Training Required:**
- 30-minute orientation call
- Video walkthrough (10 minutes)
- Email FAQ document
- Access to help hotline

**Timeline:**
- Month 1: Initial outreach + agreement
- Month 2: Materials creation + approval
- Month 3-4: Boot drive execution
- Month 5: Results publication

**Recognition:**
- Website listing of participating departments
- Monthly newsletter feature (if in top 10)
- Social media shoutouts
- Annual first responder appreciation event invitation

**Expected Results Per Department:**
- $1,500 average contribution
- 60% to pediatric charities = $900 per drive
- 2,000+ social media impressions
- Local media coverage (1-2 mentions)

**National Scale (100 departments × 1-2 drives/year):**

- Coordinate campaigns
- Build relationships
- Plan joint events
- Celebrate wins

- 10:30-11:30: Breakout sessions (by profession or topic)
- 11:30-12:30: Coordinated planning (next quarter campaigns)
- 12:30: Lunch + informal networking

**Locations:**
- Regional hub (central location for all departments)
- Rotating among participating departments

**Hosting:**
- FOR THE KIDS covers cost (facility rental, food, materials)

**Expected Outcomes:**

- Movement building

**National Scale (10 regions):**
