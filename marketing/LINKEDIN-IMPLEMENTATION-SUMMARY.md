## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Low engagement rate
**Mitigation:**
- Use proven templates (tested patterns)
- Adjust timing based on analytics
- Test new post formats weekly
- A/B test hashtags

### Risk 2: Connection acceptance < 25%
**Mitigation:**
- Personalize every outreach (80%+ personalization)
- Comment on targets' posts before connecting
- Reference specific work in 1st message
- Use appropriate templates by audience

### Risk 3: Gospel compliance drift
**Mitigation:**
- Checklist before every post
- Monthly compliance audit
- Gospel V1.3 keywords built into templates
- Automated reminders

### Risk 4: Low article engagement
**Mitigation:**
- Promote via advocates (10+ shares)
- Share in all groups
- Repurpose article content into 10+ posts
- Link from every post for 2 weeks

### Risk 5: No corporate partnerships
**Mitigation:**
- B2B angle posts weekly
- CSR leader targeted outreach
- Partnership template ready (one-pager)
- Early conversations tracked systematically
