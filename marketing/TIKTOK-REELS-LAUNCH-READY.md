## SECTION 7: DUET & STITCH ENGAGEMENT TACTICS

### Understanding Duets (TikTok/Instagram Reels)

**What is a Duet?**
- Side-by-side video feature where you respond to another creator's video
- Creates conversation, increases reach for both videos
- Algorithm favors Duets (engagement metric = valuable)
- Your duet appears on both accounts' For You Pages

**Duet Strategy:**

**A. Duet Backers Who Mention You**
1. Set up notification for mentions/tags
2. When someone posts duet saying "I backed FOR THE KIDS because..."
3. Duet them back (shows gratitude + community building)
4. This creates viral loop: Their friends see your duet ? More backers
5. Expected reach multiplier: 2x per duet reciprocation

**B. Create Duet Prompts (Viral Hooks)**
1. Post: "Duet me if you're backing FOR THE KIDS"
2. Post: "Duet me with YOUR reason to care about kids' healthcare"
3. Post: "Duet me and finish this sentence: 'I backed FOR THE KIDS because...'"
4. Purpose: Capture user-generated content + proof of community support
5. Re-share best duets in Stories/posts (gives them visibility)

**C. Duet Industry Influencers**
1. Identify TikTok creators in: healthtech, startups, philanthropy, AI
2. Duet their relevant content (not their request for duets)
3. Add your FOR THE KIDS angle
4. Example: Duet "top healthtech innovations" + add "Gospel V1.3 changed the game"
5. Expected outcome: 10-15% of their followers see your content

**D. Duet Competitors (Ethical Comparison)**
1. Find TikTok videos about corporate charity (generic 1% contribution content)
2. Duet with comparison angle
3. Keep respectful (don't mock, just show contrast)
4. Example: Duet "Company gives 2% to charity" ? "We give 60%"
5. Algorithm boost: Duets that challenge/discuss get 3x reach

**Best Duet Timing:**

- **Font Families:** Bold sans-serif only (Montserrat, Poppins, Inter, Impact)

- **Maximum Size:** 120pt (don't block video)
- **Weight:** Bold (700+) for readability

- **Text Color:** High contrast only (white on dark OR dark on light)
- **No gradients** (hard to read on small screens)
- **Background:** Semi-transparent black or white (if overlaying video)
- **WCAG AA Compliance:** All text must have 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum

*Duration & Positioning:*
- **Minimum Duration:** 2-3 seconds per text overlay
- **Positioning:** Center screen (avoid corners where TikTok UI appears)
- **Safe Zone:** Keep text within center 80% of frame (account for UI bars)
- **Animation:** Fade in/out (avoid flashing for photosensitivity safety)

**Audio Requirements:**

*Trending Sounds:*
- **Source:** TikTok native sound library or Instagram audio library (royalty-free)

*Voiceover (if applicable):*
- **Quality:** Clear, professional audio (no background noise)
- **Microphone:** Lavalier mic (Rode VideoMic Me, $60) or smartphone mic in quiet room
- **Post-Processing:** Light compression, noise reduction
- **Pacing:** Slower than normal speech (text overlay reading time)
