## Moderation Policy

### Our Moderation Philosophy

**Moderators serve the mission, not personal preferences.**

- Moderators enforce Gospel V1.3 compliance

- Moderators maintain professional, respectful environment

1. **Friendly Correction** - First offense, clear mistake

## Reporting & Safety

### How to Report Issues

**Minor Issues (Language violations, off-topic posts):**
- Use Discord's built-in report feature
- Message any online moderator
- Post in #support

**Major Issues (Harassment, child safety, security):**
- Email: safety@aidoesitall.website
- Include: Screenshots, usernames, timestamps
- Mark as URGENT if child safety related

- Email: security@aidoesitall.website

### What Happens After a Report

1. **Acknowledgment** - Within 24 hours
2. **Investigation** - Review evidence, interview parties if needed
3. **Decision** - Apply appropriate penalty per escalation path
4. **Communication** - Notify reporter of outcome (privacy permitting)
5. **Documentation** - Log incident for pattern tracking

### Moderator Response Times

- **Critical (child safety, active harassment):** Immediate
- **High (Gospel violations, spam):** Within 1 hour
- **Medium (off-topic, language):** Within 4 hours
- **Low (general questions):** Within 24 hours

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: What is FOR THE KIDS?**
A: A for-profit tech platform that automatically allocates 60% of all revenue to Verified Pediatric Charities, using AI and blockchain for transparent, trustless execution.

**Q: Is this a charity or nonprofit?**
A: No. We are a for-profit LLC (Trash or Treasure Online Recycler LLC, EIN: 33-4655313) with automatic beneficiary allocation built into our revenue model.

**Q: Can I get a tax deduction for purchases?**
A: No. Since this is not a charity and you're purchasing products/services (not donating), purchases are not tax-deductible.

**Q: How do I know the 60% really goes to kids?**
A: All profit allocations are tracked in our PostgreSQL database and will be verifiable on-chain once our smart contract is deployed. Quarterly financial reports are published in #dao-treasury.

### Participation Questions

**Q: How do I advance to higher community tiers?**
A: Consistently contribute value, help others, maintain Gospel compliance, and demonstrate mission alignment. Moderators track contributions and promote members.

**Q: Can I promote my business/project here?**
A: Only if it directly advances the FOR THE KIDS mission. Post in #partnerships for review. Pure self-promotion will be removed.

**Q: How do I become a moderator?**
A: Active, trusted members may be invited by current moderators. You cannot apply directly. Focus on being a great community member first.

**Q: Can I create unofficial FOR THE KIDS content?**
A: Yes, with proper disclosures and Gospel compliance. Contact marketing@aidoesitall.website for brand assets. Major campaigns require pre-approval.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Where is the smart contract deployed?**
A: Smart contract deployment is in development. Current profit allocation is enforced through backend logic in api/server.js.

**Q: Can I contribute code to the platform?**
A: Yes. The platform is Claude-maintained, but community contributions are reviewed. All code must be generated or approved by Claude instances per Gospel Rule #1.

**Q: How can I integrate with the FOR THE KIDS API?**
A: API documentation is available in #technical. Contact api@aidoesitall.website for API keys and integration support.
