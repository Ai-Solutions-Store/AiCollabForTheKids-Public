## Universal YouTube Shorts Best Practices

### Formatting Guidelines
- **Title Length:** Under 60 characters (optimized for mobile)
- **Description Hook:** First line MUST grab attention (shows in preview)
- **CTA Placement:** Middle or end of description (after value delivery)
- **Hashtags:** 5-8 relevant tags (don't spam)
- **Tags:** 10-15 searchable keywords

### Thumbnail Requirements for Shorts
- **Text:** Large, bold, high contrast (white/yellow on dark void #141413)
- **Font:** Space Grotesk or similar (brand compliant)
- **Readability:** Must be readable on 3-inch phone screen
- **Color Palette:** Use brand colors (Coral #CC785C, Blue #078EFA, Teal #20808D, Gold #F4B400)

### Gospel V1.3 Compliance Checklist
- **Revenue Split:** Always "60%" (never "50%")
- **Terminology:** Use "revenue," "profit allocation," "purchase" (NEVER "donation" or "escrow")
- **Brand Voice:** Dark tech aesthetic, benevolent technocracy positioning
- **Mission First:** Every video must reference the kids/pediatric charities

### Posting Schedule Recommendation
- **Frequency:** 1 Short per day (5-day campaign)
- **Best Times:** 12pm EST, 6pm EST (peak engagement)
- **Pinned Comment:** Link to Kickstarter with additional context
- **Community Tab:** Cross-promote each Short

### Engagement Hooks
Each video should trigger one of these psychological responses:
1. **Curiosity:** "AI overruled the founder? How?"
2. **Verification:** "Can I actually check the blockchain?"
3. **Trust:** "No humans touched this - it's pure AI"
4. **Impact:** "60% actually goes to kids"
