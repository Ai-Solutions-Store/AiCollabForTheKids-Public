# MEDIA KIT CHECKLIST FOR THE KIDS
**Complete List of Assets & Materials to Prepare for Press Coverage**

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This checklist ensures you have all necessary materials ready before distributing press releases. Journalists will request these assets, and having them pre-prepared speeds up story placement significantly.

**Preparation Timeline:** Complete all items 1 week before press distribution
**Total Items:** 45+ assets and documents
**Estimated Preparation Time:** 20-30 hours
**Recommended Review:** Once monthly, update outdated materials
