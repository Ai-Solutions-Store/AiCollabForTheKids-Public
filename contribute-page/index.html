Frequently Asked Questions

Where exactly does my money go?

60% goes directly to verified pediatric charities (currently Shriners Children's Hospital). 30% funds infrastructure (servers, AI APIs, development). 10% goes to the founder for sustainable operations. This split is immutable and hardcoded.

Is this tax-deductible?

This is a contribution to a for-profit tech platform with a charitable mission, not a direct charitable donation. While 60% goes to verified 501(c)(3) charities, your contribution itself may not be tax-deductible. Consult your tax advisor for specific guidance.

How can I verify the split?

All transactions are tracked transparently. The Gospel V1.3 revenue split is hardcoded in our platform's dao-logic.js file and protected by AI governance. Receipts are stored immutably on AWS S3 and can be audited.

Which charities do you support?

We currently partner with Shriners Children's Hospital. As we grow, we'll add more verified pediatric charities. All partner charities must be verified 501(c)(3) organizations focused on pediatric care.

Can I contribute monthly instead?

Yes! For recurring monthly contributions, visit our Founding Members page where you can subscribe and get access to our AI platform while supporting the mission.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (via Square), PayPal, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDC). All payments are processed securely through industry-standard payment processors.

Can I get a refund?

All contributions are final. However, if there's a processing error or duplicate charge, contact us at joshlcoleman@gmail.com and we'll resolve it immediately.

How is this different from just donating to charity directly?

Great question! By supporting our platform, you're enabling sustainable AI-powered solutions that generate ongoing revenue for kids. Instead of a one-time donation, you're helping build a system that creates perpetual value for children's charities through technology.
